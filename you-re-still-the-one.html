15. 2. reign Read the following dialog. a. b. Titus: Do you like the white shirt or the blue shirt? Tulus I prefer the white one. Titus: Really? I like the blue one better. Tulus I think the white one looks better with your beige pants. Titus: Maybe, but I still prefer the blue one. Mention the expression of stating opinion in the dialog. What does the utterance "I prefer the white one" mean? Answer: ​


Jawaban dari pertanyaan-pertanyaan di atas adalah sebagai berikut:

a. Expression of stating opinion from the dialogue is:

"I prefer the white one."

"I like the blue one better."

"I think the white one looks better with your beige pants."

"I still prefer the blue one."

b. It means that the speaker speaks their opinion about their preference.

Pembahasan

When stating opinion, people tend to start their sentence with:

"I think..."

"In my opinion..."

"I thought..."

"From my point of view..."

But not only that, we can also state our opinion and preference using "I like..." or "I prefer..."

20. remember-the -you-still-do-lesson-this?


Jawaban:

Do you still remember this lesson? / Do you still remember the lesson?

Perbedaan dari 2 kalimat tsb:

Objek kalimat 1 = this lesson (pelajaran ini - lebih spesifik)

objek kalimat 2 = the lesson (pelajarannya - secara umum)

Kenapa jadinya 2 kaliamat?

Karena dalam jumbled words yang telah diberi kita mendapat 2 kata keterangan. 'the' sebagai determiner, dan 'this' sebagai pronoun.

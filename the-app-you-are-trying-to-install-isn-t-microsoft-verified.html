14. Please change it into negative and interrogative sentence.We are trying to bake the cake.You are going to the zoo today.Lisa and Boxi are flying to Mexico next week.​


Jawaban:

1.

(+) We are trying to bake the cake.

(-) We are not trying to bake the cake.

(?) Are we trying to bake the cake?

2.

(+) You are going to the zoo today.

(-) You are not going to the zoo today.

(?) Are you going to the zoo today?

3.

(+) Lisa and Boxi are flying to Mexico next week.

(-) Lisa and Boxi are not flying to Mexico next week.

(?) Are Lisa and Boxi flying to Mexico next week?

Penjelasan:

Present Continuous Tense

(+) Subject + is/am/are + Verb-ing

example :

He is studying math.

They are studying math.

I am studying math.

(-) Subject + is/am/are + not + Verb-ing

example :

He isn't studying math.

They aren't studying math.

I amnot studying math.

(?) Is/am/are + Subject + Verb-ing + ?

example :

Is he studying math?

Are they studying math?

Am I studying math?

signal words :

right now, at the moment

Note :

is digunakan oleh he-she-it

are digunakan oleh you-we-they

am digunakan oleh I

--------------------------------------------------------

Mapel : English

Materi : Present Continuous Tense

• PRESENT CONTINUOUS TENSE •

ditinjau dari pilihan jawabannya, ada 2 kemungkinan tense pd kalimat yaitu present continuous tense (S + tobe + v-ing) atau simple present tense (S + V1)

→ jika kalimatnya berupa present continuous tense, maka jawabannya yaitu B. Is meowing, are trying
• menggunakan 'is' karena subjectnya tunggal (hanya 'cat' saja tdk pakai 's')
• menggunakan 'are' karena subjectnya plural (we yang artinya kami / subject jamak)

→ jika kalimatnya berupa simple present, maka yg benar yaitu
« meows, try »
Namun pilihan ini tdk terdapat di pilihan jawaban

✐ kalimat soal tsb merupakan present continuous tense, dan jawaban yg benar yaitu

25. You want to use App Engine, but you will need to use a custom runtime. Which of the following the best option? a. App Engine Flexible b. Google Kubernetes Engine c. App Engine Internal d. App Engine Standard​


Jawaban:

a. App Engine Flexible

Penjelasan:

The best option for using a custom runtime on App Engine is App Engine Flexible. App Engine Flexible allows you to use custom runtimes based on Docker containers, providing more flexibility and control over the runtime environment. Google Kubernetes Engine (GKE) is a container orchestration platform that can be used to deploy and manage containers, but it is not specifically designed for App Engine. App Engine Internal and App Engine Standard do not provide the same level of flexibility for custom runtimes as App Engine Flexible.

Commonly Confused Words Used to vs Be used to

I used to walk to school.

I used to listen to pop music.

I used to drink milk in the morning.

I am used to cycling to school.

I am used to listening to rock music.

I am used to drinking coffee in the morning.

-----------

Explanation

Used to adalah phrasal verb yang digunakan untuk mengekspresikan suatu kebiasaan di masa lalu yang tidak lagi dilakukan. Used to diikuti bare infinitive.

Be used to digunakan untuk mengekspresikan sesuatu yang telah menjadi kebiasaan/normal, atau menjadi suatu yang biasa dilakukan padahal sebelumnya tidak. Be used to diikuti gerund.

Bandingkan

When I was in Indonesia, I used to drive on the left side of the road. (Saya tidak lagi di Indonesia, dan saya tidak lagi menyetir di sisi kiri)

I'm from Indonesia. I am used to driving on the left side of the road. (Saya dari Indonesia jadi saya terbiasa menyetir di sisi kiri).

32. on a debate, a speaker said that the next general election will cause sosial crash. you do not agree with the opinion. what do you say.?


EXPRESSION AGREEMENT/DISAGREEMENT

Dalam kehidupan sehari-hari kita menggunakan expression opinion yaitu ekspresi untuk menyatakan pendapat/opini kita terhadap sesuatu hal.

Sebagai reaksi terhadap expression opinion adalah agreement atau disagreement.

Phrase yang sering digunakan dalam ekspresi ini adalah .

Expression Agreement

I totally agree with you.That's a good idea.I am totally with you.Yes, you right.I support your idea

Expression Disagreement

I don't think so.I'm afraid I'm disagree with you.That's not always true.I'd say the exact opposite.No. I'm not sure about that.

Expression Giving Opinion

In my opinionI believe (that)I am certain (that)I am strongly believeI am sureI think..

Setelah kita belajar mengenai ekpresi-ekspresi diatas, mari kita masuk dalam pembahasan soal yang diberikan.

Pembahasan

On a debate, a speaker said that the next general election will cause social crash.  You do not agree with the opinion. What do you say?

Answer: I don't agree with your opinion. The government, media and social media should help to give political education to the people and help to build a fair and free election,  so they can trust the government who runs the election. With a free and fair election, we can avoid the social crash.

11. QuizzSantiago memiliki 20 Penghapus, 10 Penggaris, 15 Pensil. jika penggaris diambil secara acak, maka Peluang terambilnya Pensil adalah ....​


Peluang terambilnya penggaris = 2/9Peluang terambilnya pensil = 1/3

Pendahuluan

Peluang / Probabilitas dalam Matematika adalah cara yang digunakan untuk mengetahui kemungkinan terjadinya sebuah peristiwa tertentu.

Istilah dalam peluang :

Ruang sampel = Himpunan semua hasil yang mungkin terjadi.Titik sampel = Anggota ruang sampel.Kejadian = Himpunan bagian ruang sampel.

Peluang bisa dirumuskan :

[tex]\sf \large \: p \: = \frac{banyaknya \: hasil \: yang \: dimaksud}{banyaknya \: hasil \: yang \: mungkin}[/tex]

Atau :

[tex]\sf \large \: p \: = \frac{n \: ( \: a \: )}{n \: ( \: s \: )}[/tex]

Diketahui

Santiago memiliki :

20 penghapus10 penggaris15 pensil

Ditanya

Jika penggaris diambil secara acak, maka peluang terambilnya pensil adalah ?

JawabPeluang penggaris = 2/9Peluang pensil = 1/3

Penyelesaian

Soal tidak jelas, maka :

a.l Peluang terambilnya penggaris :

n ( S ) = 20 + 10 + 15

= 45

n ( A ) = 10

Peluang terambilnya penggaris :

[tex]\sf \ \: p \: = \frac{n \: ( \: a \: )}{n \: ( \: s \: )}[/tex]

[tex]\sf \: p \: = \frac{10}{45}[/tex]

Sederhanakan :

[tex]\sf \huge p \: = \frac{2}{9} [/tex]

Peluang terambilnya penggaris = 2/9

b.) Peluang terambilnya pensil :

n ( S ) = 20 + 10 + 15

= 45

n ( A ) = 15

Peluang terambilnya pensil :

[tex]\sf \ \: p \: = \frac{n \: ( \: a \: )}{n \: ( \: s \: )}[/tex]

[tex]\sf \large \: p \: = \frac{15}{45}[/tex]

Sederhanakan :

[tex]\sf \huge \: p \: = \frac{1}{ 3} [/tex]

Peluang terambilnya pensil = 1/3

KesimpulanPeluang terambilnya penggaris = 2/9Peluang terambilnya pensil = 1/3

Subject : English

Category : Reading comprehension

Level : SHS

Code : 5

------

Answers :

A. reading for getting specific information  

1. Santiago Uno is going to stay in Garuda Hotel.

2. Krishna Kumar is going to see a doctor tomorrow.

3. Santiago Uno reserved two twin rooms in Garuda Hotel.

4. Krishna kumar made an appointment with dr Gunawan.

B. Reading for getting the main ideas of the text.

1. What is the first dialogue about?

It is about making a hotel room reservation by phone.

2. What is the second dialogue about?

It is about making a doctor appointment by phone.

C. Reading for getting similar meaning from contextual clues. Find in the dialogue the phrases which mean the same.

1. Hang on = hold on

2. With pleasure = you're welcome

3. Pardon? = sorry?

4. That's okay = that's fine

D. Reading for detailed information

1. Why did the receivers ask the callers to repeat theirs names?

Because they did not hear the names properly the first time.

2. Why did Santiago Uno make a call?

Because he wanted to reserve a room at Garuda Hotel.

3. Why did Krishna Kumar make a call?

Because he wanted to make an appointment to see a doctor at the clinic.

E. Reading between the lines

1. Why does the receptionist thank Mr. Uno for choosing the Garuda Hotel?

Because the receptionist is happy that Mr Uno decides to stay in the hotel.

2. At the end of dialogue, why does the receptionist repeat the time of the appointment?

To make sure Mr Kumar understand and remember the correct time of the appointment.

F. Reading for inference

1. What can we infer from the first dialogue?

We can infer that Mr Santiago Uno has made a reservation for tomorrow at Garuda Hotel. He reserves two twin rooms, breakfast excluded. The room rate is seven hundred thousand rupiahs per room per night.

2. What can we infer for the second dialogue?

We can infer that Mr Khrisna Kumar is going to see dr Gunawan at Twenty Four Seven Clinic. The appointment is at half past five tomorrow afternoon.

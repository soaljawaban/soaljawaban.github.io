4. 1.cane toads...(eliminate) a lot of species if their number...(not go) down.2.sit still! the cat ... (not come) to you if you...(not be) very quiet.use will and correct verbs in brackets (future conditional)


Subject : English
Category :  Conditional Sentence (type 1)

The First Conditional is used for possible situations. These situations are very likely to happen in the future when certain conditions are fulfilled. The conditions are realistic.

Structure :
If + Simple Present, Simple Future
(If + S + V1/Vs, S + will + V1)

ANSWERS :

1. Cane toads will eliminate a lot of species if their number does not go down.

2. Sit still! The cat won't come to you if you are not very quiet.

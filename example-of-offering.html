29. 1. What is offering help?2. What is the function of offering help?3. What expression are used in;a. Asking an offering helpb. Giving an offering help.c. Responding to offering help4. Write a short example of dialogue using an offering he​


Offering help is an expression of voluntary act to lend someone a hand.The function of offering help is to propose or provide help for others.Here are the list of expressions used in:(a) Asking for helpCan you...?Could you...?Would you...?(b) Offering helpWould you like me to...?May I help you?Can we help you?Can I lend you a hand with that?What can we do for you?Should I help you?Shall we...?(c) Responding to help offerAccepting: Thank youAccepting: I really appreciate it.Accepting: Yes, please.Accepting: Yes, it's very kind of you.Accepting: Thanks, that would be lovely.Refusing: No, thank you.Refusing: Thanks, but you don't need to.Refusing: I appreciate it, but I can do it myself.Refusing: Don't worry, I am okay to do it alone.Refusing: No need, thanks anyway.

 4. Dialogue asking for and offering help

-At a coffee shop-

Server: Hello good morning, may I help you?

Customer: Yes, please. Could you tell me what is the best coffee here?

Server: Certainly. For mornings people usually order a Caffe Americano or Caffe Latte.

Customer: Okay, I would like to order a Caffe Latte, please.

Server: What size do you want?

Customer: A large, please. Oh and with an extra shot.

Server: Do you want it hot or cold?

Customer: Hot, please.

Server: Okay, do you want anything else?

Customer: No, that would be all, thank you.

Server: The total is Rp 29.000,00. What kind of payment do you prefer?

Customer: Cash, here you go.

Server: Please wait over there for your order, thank you so much.

Customer: Thanks.

Pembahasan

Asking for help digunakan untuk bertanya atau meminta bantuan dalam bahasa Inggris. Ketika meminta atau menawarkan bantuan dalam bahasa Inggris dengan sopan harus menggunakan kata kerja bantu (modal auxiliary verbs), yaitu:

WouldMayCanCouldShallShould

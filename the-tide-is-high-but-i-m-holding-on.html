Carnivores such as sea stars eat other animals in the tide pool, such as clams or mussels. Tide pools depend on the changing level of ocean water. Some organisms, such as seaweed, thrive in an aquatic environment, when the tide is in and the pool is full. To compare the heights of the two walls, we need to convert both heights to the same unit of measurement. Let's convert both heights to centimeters:

Wall 1: 2 m 45 cm = 245 cmWall 2: 5 m 36 cm = 536 cm

Subtracting the height of Wall 1 from Wall 2, we get:

536 cm - 245 cm = 291 cm

So, the first wall is 291 cm shorter than the second.

23. Build an adequacy/inadequacy sentence based on the clauses "The rack is high. John cannot reach"!​


An inadequacy sentence can be formed from the above clauses:

The rack is too high so John cannot reach it.

This sentence shows the inadequacy of John in reaching the rack because it is too high. The word which show the inadequacy is "too".

Pembahasan

Dua klausa di atas bisa dibentuk menjadi kalimat yang bermakna inadequacy atau ketidakmampuan. Dalam hal ini ketidakmampuan John dalam menggapai rak tersebut karena terlalu tinggi. Dalam kalimat tersebut yang menunjukkan ketidakmampuan adalah kata "too high" atau terlalu tinggi dan cannot reach artinya tidak dapat menggapai. Maka dua kata tersebut menunjukkan inadequacy yang dialami oleh John.

Some organisms, such as seaweed, thrive in an aquatic environment, when the tide is in and the pool is full.

26. buatkan percakapan 5 orang tentang asking & giving something contoh : can you give me , take me that , could you please , would you kindly . beri tema , dalam bhs.inggris, yang panjang yaa .


One beautiful day, 5 kids, Sinta, Rama, Wahyu, Nanda and Siska play together.
Sinta : Howdy Rama! Can you give me that rope? I need it to tie Wahyu.
Rama : Howdy Sinta! Why do you need to tie Wahyu?
Sinta : I need it because Wahyu keep bothering me as a sheriff. Could you please pass me that rope now?
Rama : Huh fine, but you need to give me something for exchange. How about a cup of hot coffee?
Sinta : Deal.
Nanda : Howdy there! What are you, my friends, doing in this wonderful day?
Sinta : Howdy Nanda! We're playing sheriff and cowboys. Too bad, Wahyu be the bad cowboy. Would you kindly help me tie Wahyu?
Nanda : You're funny Sinta, but sorry i can't. My beautiful hair won't let me do that you know. Okay ta-ta!
Sinta : Uh that girl  with her beautiful hair. Fine then.
Suddenly, Siska met Sinta that day.
Siska : Howdy Sinta!
Sinta : Howdy Siska!
Siska : What are you going to do with that rope?
Sinta : Well, i'm going to tie Wahyu because he's a bad cowboy. Would you lend me a hand to tie him?
Siska : Sure, just give me a minute to finish my tea.
Siska and Sinta sneak behind Wahyu and they both ties Wahyu really good.
Wahyu : Hey! Please, would you kindly let me go? I promise that i won't be a bad cowboy again. Please.
Siska : Well, i might think that he is worth for your mercy Sinta, what do you think?
Sinta : Okay, i'll let you go.
Wahyu : Great, thanks! Uh could you please untie me now?
Sinta and Siska : No!
They both laugh for awhile and finally untie Wahyu. Wahyu become a good cowboy from that moment.

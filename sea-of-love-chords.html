11. the three basic chords in _____ the tonic, the dominant, and the subdominant.


The three basic chords in functional harmony are the tonic, the dominant, and the subdominant.

Pembahasan

Soal tersebut merupakan contoh penggunaan Simple Present Tense, rumusnya (Subject + is/am/are + noun/adjective).

Contoh:

The Sky is blue They are so excited

Jawaban yang tepat untuk mengisi kalimat tersebut adalah functional harmony are, karena kalimat selanjutnya adalah objek jamak (plural) yaitu the tonic, the dominant, and the subdominant. Maka harus diisi dengan subjek dan kata kerja (verb) yang sesuai.

Soal ini kurang lengkap karena sebenarnya memiliki beberapa pilihan yaitu:

a. Functional harmony

b. Functional harmony is

c. Functional harmony are

d. Functional harmony they are

Pilihan yang tepat adalah c. Functional harmony are, karena sesuai dengan subjeknya yang jamak.

Pilihan lainnya kurang tepat karena kalimatnya tidak sesuai dengan subjek dan objek yang menyertai.

16. What is the volume of the Philippine Sea​


Jawaban:

The Philippine Sea is a marginal sea of the Western Pacific Ocean east of the Philippine archipelago (hence the name), occupying an estimated surface area of 5 million square kilometers (2×106 sq mi).[1] The Philippine Sea Plate forms the floor of the sea.[2] It is bordered by the first island chain to the west, which comprises the Ryukyu Islands in the northwest and Taiwan in the due west, and the Filipino islands of Luzon, Catanduanes, Samar, Leyte and Mindanao to the southwest; by the Japanese islands of Honshu, Shikoku and Kyūshū to the north; by the second island chain to the east, which comprises the Bonin Islands and Iwo Jima in the northeast, the Mariana Islands (including Guam, Saipan and Tinian) in the due east, and Halmahera, Palau, Yap and Ulithi (of the Caroline Islands) in the southeast; and by Indonesia's Morotai Island to the south.[3]

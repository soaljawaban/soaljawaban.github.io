15. I do not like playing chess (+)...... (-).......


(-)I do not like playing chess

(+)I like playing chess

(+)do i like playing chess ?

Pembahasan

rumus simple present tense sebagai berikut :

Kalimat positifSubject + verb 1 (+ s/es) + object/keteranganSubject + to be (am/is/are)/ adjective/adverb

Kalimat negatifSubjek + do/does + not + Verb 1 + Objek/keteranganSubject + to be (am/is/are) + not + adjective/adverb

Kalimat tanyaDo/does + Subjek + Verb 1 + objek/keterangan?To be (am/is/are) + subject + adjective/adverb?

---------------------------------------------Berikut ini,Contoh Kalimat Simple Present Tense:

=> Contoh 1

(+) He likes Apple

(-) He doesn’t like Apple

(?) Does he like Apple ?

=> Contoh 2

(+) You speak English

(-) You don’t speak English

(?) Do you speak English ?

--------------------------------------------Keterangan waktu Present Tense

=> Everyday ( setiap hari)

=> every morning (setiap pagi)

=> usually (biasanya)

=> Sometimes (kadang-kadang)

=> always (selalu)

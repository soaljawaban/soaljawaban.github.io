Original Visto, Sterile Eye DropsINDICATIONS:Work effectively to relieve the redness of eye or itchdue to minor irritation that is caused of dust, smoke,after swimming, and so on.DIRECTIONS FOR USE:Drop 1 - 2 drops for each eye 2 - 3 times a day or based on doctor's advice or receipt.DIRECTIONS FORSTORAGE:Stores in the dry places under 30°C temperature.Close the container and keep from pollution.​4. when can we use visto? A. Our eyes are healthy. B. Our eyes get mayor irritation C. Our eyes are redness. D. Our eyes are in bad irritation.


The answer is B

Because it is stated in the text that Insto work effectively to relive the redness of eye or itch due to minor irritation that is caused of dust, smoke, after swimming, and so on.

Jawaban:

4. C. Our eyes are redness

Penjelasan:

Pertanyaannya, kapan kita bisa pakai visto?

opsi A. mata kita sehat, (kalau sehat dan baik baik aja, untuk apa kita pake obat tetes mata? - yang gunanya untuk mengobati, padahal mata kita sehat).

opsi B. mayor irritation itu artinya dalam jumlah yang besar. Padahal visto digunakan untuk minor irritation (iritasi ringan), tertulis jelas di indications.

opsi C. "work effectively to relieve the redness of eyes or itch..." digunakan untuk mata merah karena iritasi ringan.

opsi D. mata kita dalam kondisi iritasi yang parah, (indikasi hanya untuk iritasi ringan, kalau iritasi nya parah kita bisa pergi ke dokter mata)

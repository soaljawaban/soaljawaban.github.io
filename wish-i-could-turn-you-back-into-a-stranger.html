contoh percakapan:

"I wish I were a princess"

A: "I wish I were a princess."

B: "Ah, the allure of living in a grand castle, wearing elegant gowns, and being treated like royalty."

A: "Exactly! I would love to experience the royal life, attend glamorous balls, and have my own fairy tale ending."

B: "Being a princess sounds like a dream come true, but it's not all about glitz and glamour. It also comes with great responsibilities and expectations."

A: "I understand that it's not all roses, but I can't help imagining myself in a beautiful tiara, ruling over a kingdom."

B: "Well, with your kind heart and determination, you'd make a wonderful princess. But remember, being true to yourself is essential, no matter where life takes you."

A: "You're right. Perhaps the best thing I can do is carry the values of a princess in my heart and make a positive impact on the world around me."

B: "That's a fantastic attitude! Remember, you don't need a title to make a difference and be a source of inspiration for others."

A: "Thank you for the encouragement. I'll strive to be the best version of myself, whether I'm a princess or not."

"I wish I could fly"

A: "I wish I could fly."

B: "Wouldn't that be amazing? Imagine soaring through the skies like a bird, feeling the wind beneath your wings."

A: "Yes, it would be incredible. I'd travel to all the places I've ever dreamed of, and the view from up there must be breathtaking."

B: "Absolutely! I can picture it now – flying over lush forests, gliding above vast oceans, and witnessing stunning sunsets from high above the clouds."

A: "And think about all the time I could save on my daily commute. No more traffic jams or long journeys."

B: "True, it would be a convenient superpower to have. But remember, with great power comes great responsibility!"

2. I hope I get a chance to meet your boss.

3. I hope I am able to attend the meeting.

4. I hope you are able to save my life.

5. I hope she agrees to be my girlfriend.

Penjelasan:

The formula for making hope sentences from wish sentences is to use the phrase "I hope" followed by a verb in the present tense that expresses the wish. For example, "I wish I had more money" becomes "I hope I have more money."

(–) We cannot understand it easily if we don't do it together(?) Can we understand it easily if we do it together?

(+) He can also go down easily.

(–) He cannot also go down easily. (?) Can he also go down easily?

(+) Birds can fly because they have wings

(–) Birds cannot fly because they don't have wings(?) Can birds fly because they have wings?

(–) We cannot fly because we don't have wings.

(+) We can fly because we have wings(?) Can we fly because we have wings?

Pattern:

(+) S + can + V1 + O(–) S + can + not + V1 + O(?) Can + S + V1 + O + ?

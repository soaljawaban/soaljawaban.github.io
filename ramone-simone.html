21. Pay attention to the verbs in the biography of Johny Ramone. All the verbs are written in the past    tense. Do you know why?​


Because we are describing events of the past, the obvious option for autobiographical writing would be the past tense. Present tense is a riskier stylistic choice (especially for memoir) but can be very effective in creating a sense of excitement and immediacy.

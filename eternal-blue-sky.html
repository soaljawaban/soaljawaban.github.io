37. The sky ... our heads was deep blue. A. BelowB. AboveC. OutsideD. Under​


The sky above our heads was deep blue.

PREFACE

Preposition of place digunakan untuk menyatakan letak tempat. Jenis jenisnya :

onaboveunderbelowoutsideinsideetc

➡ Contoh :

The cat is on the table.

[Kucing ada di atas meja. ]

The cat is under the chair.

[Kucing ada di bawah kursi. ]

EXPLICATION

The sky ... our heads was deep blue. 

[Langit yang ada di atas kepala kita berwarna biru tua. ]

A. Below => Di bawah

B. Above => Di atas

C. Outside => Di luar

D. Under => Di bawah

Sehingga yang tepat adalah yang B (above).

Hope they help you ^^)

Grade : VIII

Subject : English

Topic : Preposition

Category : 7.5

Keywords : Preposition of place

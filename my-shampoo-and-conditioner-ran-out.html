Pembahasan

The meaning of the phrasal verb:

A. Ran into = cost (harga), meet by accident (bertemu dengan tidak sengaja)

B. Ran over = explain quickly (menerangkan dengan cepat), hit with a vehicle (tertabrak kendaraan)

C. Ran down =

Hit a pedestrian with a vehicle (menabrak seorang pejalan kaki dengan kendaraan)Lose energy or power (kehabisan tenaga)Criticise, disparage (mengkritik)Find the source or origin of something (mencari sumber atau asal sesuatu)

D. Ran out = Have none left (habis)

35. Last night I had (have) an interesting experience. At 7 p.m. I went ( ..) to visit my friend, Jim. We had (... ) supper together. At 11 p.m. I decided (...) to go home. At home, I wrote (.) in my diary, brushed (...) my teeth, and went (.) to bed. Suddenly, I heard (...) a noise outside. I got up ... ) and went (...) to close the window.I looked ()out of the window and saw (...) two men with heavy bags in their backs.Pt on (.) my clothes and went out (..). They saw (..) me and ran (...). I ran (...) after them, but they aisappeared (.). I went back (...) home. This morning, I met (.) my neighbor, Sam. He said )some thieves entered ( ... ) his house and stole (..) his wife's jewelry and other valuable things at about twelve last night.​


Dibawah ini merupakan bentuk pertama dari kata kerja bentuk kedua yang ada pada teks!

Last night I had (have) an interesting experience. At 7 p.m. I went (go) to visit my friend, Jim. We had (have) supper together. At 11 p.m, I decided (decide) to go home. At home, I wrote (write) in my diary, brushed (brush) my teeth, and went (go) to bed. Suddenly, I heard (hear) a noise outside. I got up (get) and went (go) to close the window.I looked (look) out of the window and saw (see) two men with heavy bags in their backs. Put on (put) my clothes and went out (go). They saw (see) me and ran (run). I ran (run) after them, but they disappeared (dissapear). I went back (go) home. This morning, I met (meet) my neighbor, Sam. He said (say) some thieves entered (enter) his house and stole (steal) his wife's jewelry and other valuable things at about twelve last night.

Pembahasan

Pada teks diatas kita diminta untuk mengubah kata kerja pada teks lampau menjadi kata kerja bentuk pertama. Teks diatas menceritakan kejadian yang terjadi dimasa lampau sehingga tenses yang digunakan adalah simple past tense. Berikut adalah rumus dari simple past tense:

Kalimat verbal (kalimat yang mengandung kata kerja di dalamnya).

(+) S + V2 + O.

(-) S + did + not + V1 + O.

(?)  Did + S + V1 + O ?

Pada kalimat negatif dan kalimat tanya kata kerja yang awalnya menggunakan kata kerja bentuk kedua diubah menjadi kata kerja bentuk pertama sebab sudah terdapat “did” yang merupakan bentuk lampau dari “do”.

Kalimat nominal (kalimat yang tidak mengandung kata kerja)

(+) S + tobe (was,were) + C.

(-) S + tobe (was/were) + not + C.

(?)  Tobe (was/were) + S + C + ?

Keterangan

Penggunaan tobe tergantung pada subjeknya.

Was digunakan untuk subjek I, she, he dan it.

Were digunakan untuk you, we dan they.

C= complement (keterangan, bias berupa kata sifat, tempat, dll).

O= objek

S= subjek

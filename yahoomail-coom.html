9. Chage the following simple present tense into passive voice 1. Mom and I coom spagethi for dinner 2. Ahmad and Ilham clean the classroom after study 3. My father drives the car to the office


PASSIVE VOICE :

Rumus => S + to be (am, are, is) + V3 + by + O

1) Active : Mom and I cook spaghetti for dinner

Passive : Spaghetti is cooked by mom and me for dinner.

2) Active : Ahmad and Ilham clean the classroom after study

Passive : The classroom is cleaned by Ahmad and Ilham after study.

3) Active : My father drives the car to the office

Passive : Thr car is driven by my father to the office.

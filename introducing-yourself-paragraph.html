10. Buatlah dialog yang berisi 'Greeting, Introducing yourself, and Introducing people, Describing things, and Giving opinion', dengan tema perpustakaan.


Untuk melaksanakan sebuah dialog dibutuhkan dua orang atau lebih agar dapat berkomunikasi. Berikut adalah contoh percakapan yang berisi 'Greeting, Introducing yourself, and Introducing people, Describing things, and Giving opinion', dengan tema perpustakaan.

Louisa: Hi, good morning?

Michael: Hi, good morning! What is your name?

Louisa: My name is Louisa and you?

Michael: Nice to meet you, Louisa. My name is Michael.

Louisa: Nice to meet you too, Michael. What are you doing here?

Michael: I am looking some book to read at home. How about you?

Louisa: Actually, I am waiting for someone. Oh there she is! Michael let me introduce my friend, her name is Susan and Susan this is Michael!

Susan: Hi!

Michael: Hi!

Louisa: Well, let me guide you guys because I come here often

Michael: Okay great!

Susan: Hey what is that? Could you describe it to me please?

Louisa: Sure! That is a wireless devices to look for the types of books that we will read. So, basically if we cannot find books that we want to read, we can type the title of the book or the author there and it will tell us where the book is. It will tell us either the book has been borrowed or just there.

Michael: Wow! I think that's an amazing idea! Because we won't waste our time for looking books we want to read. Just type there and voila! you know where to get the book! Cool thing!

Pembahasan:
Dalam sebuah percakapan dibutuhkan interaksi dari dua arah sehingga komunikasi berjalan dengan baik. Jika bertemu dengan seseorang yang belum dikenal sebaiknya kita memberikan ucapan salam dan memperkenalkan diri seperti contoh dialog di atas yang mengandung ucapan salam, perkenalan diri dan memperkenalkan orang lain, menjelaskan sesuatu dan memberikan opini.

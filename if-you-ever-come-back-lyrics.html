10. 1. ...to one of these seminars? A. Do you ever come B. Did you ever come C. Have you ever come D. Has you ever come E. Are you ever coming 2. Which...., this kind of seminar or a computer based training? A. Do you prefer B. Did you prefer C. Have you preferred D. Has you prefrred E. Will you prefer


Have you ever come to one of these seminars?Which do you prefer, this kind of seminar or a computer based training?

Pembahasan

Preference adalah ungkapan untuk menyertakan suatu pilihan atau untuk menunjukkan seseorang menyukai suatu hal atau benda daripada hal lainnya.

Preference dibagi menjadi 2, yaitu:

Menyebutkan pembanding

Form:

Like + noun + better than + noun

Prefer + noun + to + noun

Would rather + verb 1 + than verb 1

Would prefer + to verb 1 + rather than + verb 1

Contoh:

She likes dancing better than singing.I prefer studying to hanging out.He would rather walk than run.He would prefer to stay at home rather than swim in the pool. Tanpa menyebutkan pembanding

Form:

Prefer + to verb 1

Prefer + noun

Would rather + verb 1

Would prefer + to verb 1

Contoh:

She prefers to dance / dancingI would rather go with you.He would prefer stay at home.

==================================

Analisis soal

1)  Have you ever come to one of these seminars?

Alasan  kata ever menunjukkan bahwa kalimat tersebut menggunakan kata kerja perfect tense. Subject You, harus diikuti oleh auxiliary have. Maka, jawaban yang tepat adalah C.

2) Which do you prefer this kind of seminar or a computer based training?

Alasan: Kata kuncinya terletak pada kata or (atau). Kalimat di atas tergolong preference. Formulanya adalah Prefer + noun + to + noun

Maka, jawaban yang tepat adalah A.

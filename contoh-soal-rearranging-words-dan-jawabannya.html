12. Arti dari: 1.Slunk 2.Pre-lunch nap 3.Hard boiled brown speckled egg 4.rearranging


Subject : English

Category : Vocabulary, translation

Level : -

Code : 5

Keywords : arti


Answers :

1. Slunk adalah bentuk past dan perfect dari slink. Artinya berjalan mengendap-endap.

contoh kalimat : He slunk stealthily.


2. Pre-lunch = before lunch = sebelum makan siang

nap = tidur siang

pre-lunch nap = tidur sebelum makan siang

Contoh kalimat : John is having his pre-lunch nap.


3. hard boiled = direbus sampai matang

brown speckled egg = telur coklat berbintik-bintik

hard-boiled brown speckled egg = telur coklat berbintik-bintik yang direbus sampai matang.

Contoh kalimat : I had a hard boiled brown speckled egg for breakfast.


4. re adalah prefix yang artinya again = lagi.

arranging bentuk present perticiple dari arrange = mengatur

rearranging = mengatur kembali

Contoh kalimat : Let's try rearranging the seating positions for the dinner party.

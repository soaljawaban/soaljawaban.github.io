2. Actor /Actress - a person that acts in a play or a movie.
3. Architect - a person that designs building and houses.
4. Author - They write books or novels.
5. Baker - They make bread and cakes and normally work in a bakery.
6. Bus driver - a person that drives buses.
7. Butcher - a person that works with meat. They cut the meat and sell it in their shop.
8. Carpenter - a person that makes things from wood including houses and furniture.
9. Chef/Cook - a person that prepared food for others, often in a restaurant or café.
10. Dentist - a person that can fix problems you have with your teeth.
11. Doctor - a person you go to see when you are ill or have some type of health problem.
12. Electrician - a person that works with electric circuits.
13. Engineer - a person who develops solutions to technical problems. They sometimes design, build, or maintain engines, machines, structures or public works.
14. Farmer - a person that works on a farm, usually with animals.

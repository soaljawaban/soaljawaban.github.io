8. Tolong ya kakaaTentukan:a. nilai x,b. panjang BE, CE, dan DE.​


Jawab:

Bagian a

[tex]\frac{BD}{AB} =\frac{BE}{BC} \\ \\\frac{BD}{BD + AD} =\frac{BE}{BE + EC}\\ \\\frac{10}{10 + 8} =\frac{2x+5}{2x+5 + 3x - 3}\\ \\\frac{10}{18} =\frac{2x+5}{5x+2}\\ \\\frac{5}{9} =\frac{2x+5}{5x+2}[/tex]

25x + 10 = 18x + 45

25x - 18x = 45 - 10

7x = 35

x = 35 : 7 = 5

BE = 2x + 5 = 2(5) + 5 = 10 + 5 = 15 cm

CE = 3x - 3 = 3(5) - 3 = 15 - 3 = 12 cm

Bagian b

[tex]\frac{BD}{AB} =\frac{DE}{AC} \\\\\frac{10}{10 + 8} =\frac{DE}{12}\\ \\\frac{10}{18} =\frac{DE}{12}\\ \\\frac{5}{9} =\frac{DE}{12}[/tex]

DE = [tex]\frac{60}{9} = 6,67 cm[/tex]

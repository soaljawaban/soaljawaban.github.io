14. kanti arrives at 6 am , sinta arrives at 6.10 am ubah ke comparative dan superlative


Bahasa Inggris
Comparison Degree

Sinta arrives early.
Kanti arrives earlier than Sinta does. (C) <=
Kanti arrives the earliest. (S) <=

Early = Awal
Earlier = Lebih Awal
Earliest = Paling Awal (+ the)

Atau bisa juga lawan katanya, yaitu 'late'.

Late = Lambat/Terlambat
Later = Lebih Lambat
Latest = Paling Lambat

Kalau pakai late, maka menjadi :

Sinta arrives later than Kanti does. (C) <=
Sinta arrives the latest. (S) <=

Kesimpulannya :

Kanti arrives earlier than Sinta does. (C)
Kanti arrives the earliest. (S)
Sinta arrives later than Kanti does. (C)
Sinta arrives the latest. (S)

35. I am a college student ubahlah ke bentuk passive voice


Jawaban :

Kalimat di atas tidak bisa di rubah ke dalam bentuk pasif.

PEMBAHASAN

Kenapa tidak bisa di ubah ke pasif? Dalm kalimat pasif tentu ada yang harus kita perhatikan pada kalimat aktifnya, yaitu :

SubjectVerbObject

Subject harus diperhatikan karena nanti ketika di kalimat pasif, subject ini akan berubah menjadi by (agent).

Verb harus diperhatikan karena kata kerja yang digunakan pada kalimat aktif akan berunah menjadi verb-3 di kalimat pasif.

Object harus diperhatikan karena pada kalimat pasifnya nanti object dikalimat aktif berubah menjadi subject.

Sedangkan untuk kalimat di atas bukanlah termasuk kalimat verbal (kalimat yang didalam nya ada kata kerja). Melainkan merupakan kalimat nominal. Mari kita perhatikan struktrur kalimat dari "I am a college student".

I => Sebagai subjectAm => Sebagai To beA college student => Sebagai object

Nah, pada kalimat ini tidak ada kata kerja nya. Sehingga tidak bisa di ubah ke bentuk pasif. Terus seperti apa kalimat yang bisa di ubah ke bentuk pasif? Berikut ini adalah contoh nya :

Active : Miranda reads some books.

Passive : Some books are read by Miranda.

38. Kanti arrives at 6 am sinta arrives at 6.10 am Trianti arrivesvat 6.30 am ubah ke comparative degree dan superlative degree


Comparative and Superlative Degree

Comparative
Kanti arrives faster than Sinta
Sinta arrives faster than Trianti
Trianti arrives slower than Sinta
Sinta arrives slower than Kanti

Superlative
Kanti arrives fastest than Sinta dan Trianti
Trianti arrives slowest than Sinta and Kanti

Semoga membantu yaSubject : English
Category : Degree of Comparison (Comparative and Superlative)
Level : JHS VII

The comparative form is used when we want to compare two things with each other.
Forms :
1. one syllable adjective/adverb + er (e.g. younger)
2. more + two or more syllables adjective/adverb  (e.g. more expensive)
3. two syllables adjective that ends with Y, LE, OW, ER + er (e.g. happier, simpler, yellower)
keyword : than

The superlative form is used when we want to compare more than two things with one another.
Forms :
1. one syllable adjective/adverb + est (e.g. youngest)
2. most + two or more syllables adjective/adverb  (e.g. most expensive)
3. two syllables adjective that ends with Y, LE, OW + est (e.g. happiest, simplest, yellowest)
keyword : the

ANSWERS :
Comparatives :
1. Kanti arrives earlier than Sinta.
2. Trianti arrives later than Sinta.
3. Sinta arrives later than Kanti but earlier than Trianti.

Superlatives:
1. Kanti arrives the earliest of the three girls.
2. Trianti arrives the latest of the three girls.

Adverbs used : early (= awal) and late (= terlambat)

37. menyusn kalimat come-very-i-be-glad-you-to-can-my-if-would-for-dinner houseplease yg tau jwb


I can be very gald if you would come to my house for dinner Menyusun kalimat :
come-very-i-be-glad-you-to-can-my-if-would-for-dinner house

Kalimat yang dapat terbentuk dari kata-kata diatas adalah :
I would be very glad if you can come to my house for dinner.

Akan tetapi kalimat diatas bukanlah kalimat yang baik. Karena kalimat tersebut merupakan conditional sentence type 2 maka seharusnya :
I would be very glad if you COULD come to my house for dinner.
(hal ini apabila CAN keliru)

atau kalimat conditional sentence type 1 :
I WILL be very glad if you can come to my house for dinner.
(hal ini jikan WOULD yang keliru)

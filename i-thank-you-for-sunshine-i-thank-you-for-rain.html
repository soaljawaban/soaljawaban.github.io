Hope and wish are the expressions used to express the expectation, your sympathy, or what you want to become.

To say what you hope in the past, you use simple past;

I hope she came on time

To say what you hope about the present, you use the present;

I hope she goes back home

To say what you hope about the future, you use the present;

I hope she has a good day in Canada ( I hope she will have a good day in Canada)

To say what you wish about the past, you use the past perfect;

I wish she had come back home savely

To say what you wish about the present, you use the present;

I wish she always be happy

To say what you wish about the future, you add would;

I wish she would be happy

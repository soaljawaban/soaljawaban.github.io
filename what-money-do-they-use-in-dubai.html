4. Present tense dari kalimat berikut1. they use that one2. we do it together3. he needs a new one4. i spend my money5. they have enough money​


Nomor 1

(+) They use that one

(–) They don't use that one

(?) Do they use that one?

Nomor 2

(+) We do it together

(–) We don't do it together

(?) Do we do it together?

Nomor 3

(+) He needs a new one

(–) He doesn't need a new one

(?) Does he need a new one?

Nomor 4

(+) I spend my money

(–) I don't spend my money

(?) Do I spend my money?

Nomor 5

(+) They have enough money

(–) They don't have enough money

(?) Do they have enough money?

ExplanationSimple Present Tense

Digunakan untuk menyatakan suatu kegiatan yang dilakukan secara berulang-ulang/berkali-kali dan atau sesuatu yang sudah menjadi suatu kebiasaan.

Pattern:

(+) S + V1 (s/es)

(–) S + do/does + not + V1

(?) Do/does + S + V1

33. Say whether these sentences are true or false, and if they are false say why. 1. When people offer money for particular goods, they indicate that a demand exists. 2. Money is usually valuable in itself. 3. People do not usually have everything they want. 4. Basic needs come before luxuries. 5. Our decisions on how to use our money show what we need most and what we are willing to do without. 6. Demand for essential commodities is always elastic.​


Jawaban:

1. True

2. False. Money is valuable only because it can be exchanged for goods and services.

3. True. People have unlimited wants but limited resources to satisfy them.

4. True. People need basic necessities such as food, water, and shelter before they can afford luxuries.

5. True. Our spending choices reflect our priorities and what we consider to be important.

6. False. The demand for essential commodities can be both elastic and inelastic, depending on the circumstances. For example, the demand for food may be inelastic because people need to eat regardless of the price, but the demand for luxury food items may be elastic because people can choose to buy cheaper alternatives.

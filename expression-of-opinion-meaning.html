1. Bagaimana membuat the expression of opinion responses dan membuat kalimat tentang expression of opinion


Asking opinion
- What do you think of...
- What your opinion about...
- what is your comment?
- how is your opinion?
- how about this?
- do you have any idea?
- please, give me your opinion?
- how about ...

Respond
- I think this/that is...
- Ini my opinion ...
- As far i know...
- Well, i must say...
- My comment is...
- I feel ...
- From my point of view...

Answers

Expressing our opinion about something is an important part of English learning. Mainly because we need to be able to express our opinion and responding to others opinion as for smoother conversations.

Expressing opinion

To express our opinions, there are some sentences that we can use to start our sentence.

Basic

- I think...

- I believe...

- I feel...

- In my opinion...

Strong & personal

- I strongly believe...

- I really think...

- As far as I’m concerned...

Weak or Hesitant

- As far as I know, ...

- This is just my opinion, but...

- I could be wrong, but...

- It seems to me that...

Responding to an opinion

There are three types of respond.

Agreeing

- I agree.

- I think so too.

- I absolutely agree.

- I couldn’t agree more.

Disagreeing

- I disagree.

- I don’t think so.

- I doubt that.

- I’m not sure about that.

Let’s see how can we use some of those expression in a conversation.

Student A: Hey, have you seen the animated movie Incredibles 2?

Student B: Yes, I watched that movie last weekend with my sister. Have you watched it?

Student A: Yeah... I watched it last night with my family. I think it’s very boring.

Student B: Really? I don’t think so. It’s very interesting and funny.

Note: Student B is disagreeing with student A.

Annie: What do you think of our new Math teacher?

Sam: I think she’s very smart and kind.

Annie: I think so too. Her explanation is very easy to understand.

Note: Annie agrees that the new Math teacher is smart and kind.

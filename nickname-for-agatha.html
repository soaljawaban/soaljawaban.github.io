> Rachel, whose nickname is Agatha, is a smart student in MIHS. Her mother, Devano, is very proud of her.

Based on the text, we can infer that **D. Rachel is a smart student in MIHS**. The text explicitly states that Rachel is a smart student in MIHS. The other options are not explicitly stated in the text.

Option A, Thomas is her family's name, is not mentioned in the text.

Option B, her mother's name is Devano, is mentioned in the text, but it does not say that Devano is her only mother.

Option C, Rachel's nickname is Agatha, is mentioned in the text, but it does not say that Agatha is her only nickname.

Therefore, the only option that can be inferred from the text is option D, Rachel is a smart student in MIHS.

35. Bu Agatha bekerja sebagai tenaga penjualanbarang-barang elektronik dengan penghasilanRp1.500.000,00 per bulan. Selain itu, Bu Agathamemperoleh upah tambahan sebesar 15% daritotal penjualan. Total penjualan Bu Agatha selamabulan Januari sebesar Rp5.000.000.00. Besarupah yang diterima oleh Bu Agatha pada bulanJanuari sebesar ...a. Rp750.000,00b. Rp1.500.000,00c.Rp2.000.000,00d.Rp2.250.000,00e. Rp2.500.000,00 tolong bantu ya:)​


Jawaban:

D (Rp 2.250.000)

Penjelasan:

Diketahui:

-Penghasilan per bulan= Rp 1.500.000

-Upah tambahan= 15% dari total penjualan

- Total penjualan bulan Januari Rp 5.000.000

- Berarti upah tambahan di Bulan Januari:

[tex]5.000.000 \times 15\% = 750.000[/tex]

Ditanya:

Berapa Total upah bulan Januari?

Total Upah= Penghasilan per Bulan + Upah tambahan

[tex]total \: upah = \: 1.500.000 + 750.000 \\ total \: upah = 2.250.000[/tex]

Total Upah= Rp 2.250.000 (D)

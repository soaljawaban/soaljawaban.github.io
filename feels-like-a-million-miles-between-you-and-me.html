23. 1.write a conversation about someone's skill or general abilities2.write a short paragraph about your hobby by using like or don't like+-ing3.write the difference between can and could4.write the difference between like and like+verb ing5.write the difference between can't and couldn't​


Jawaban:

1    Conversation about someone's skill or general abilities:

Person A: Hey, did you know that John is really good at playing the guitar?

Person B: No, I didn't know that. Is he self-taught or did he take lessons?

Person A: He took lessons for a few years and now he can play just about anything.

Person B: That's impressive. What other skills does John have?

Person A: He's also a great cook. He can make some delicious meals from scratch.

Person B: That's really cool. I would love to try some of his cooking someday.

2    Short paragraph about my hobby:

I like playing video games. I find it to be a fun and relaxing way to spend my free time. I particularly enjoy games that have good stories and engaging gameplay. I don't like games that are too repetitive or have lackluster graphics.

3    Difference between can and could:

Can is used to express ability or possibility in the present time. For example: "I can play the guitar."

Could is used to express ability or possibility in the past or in a hypothetical situation. For example: "I could play the guitar when I was younger."

4    Difference between like and like + verb-ing:

Like is used as a preposition to express preference or fondness. For example: "I like playing video games."

Like + verb-ing is used to express a temporary or ongoing action. For example: "I am enjoying playing this video game right now."

5    Difference between can't and couldn't:

Can't is used to express inability or lack of possibility in the present time. For example: "I can't play the guitar."

Couldn't is used to express inability or lack of possibility in the past. For example: "I couldn't play the guitar when I was younger."

21. Melody had 17 flowers at first. One day, Issac was feeling generous and gave her some flowers. After that, Melody gave 15 of her flowers to Rena and was left with 28 flowers. How many flowers did Issac give her?


Jawaban:

Issac gave Melody 26 flowers.

Solution:

- Melody had 17 flowers at first.

- After Issac gave her some flowers, let's say he gave her x flowers. Then Melody had 17 + x flowers.

- Melody gave 15 of her flowers to Rena and was left with 28 flowers. This means she gave away 17 + x - 15 = 2 + x flowers.

- So we have the equation 2 + x = 17 + x - 28.

- Solving for x, we get x = 26.

Therefore, Issac gave Melody 26 flowers.

let's say Issac gave her x flowers, then :

17+ x −15 = 28
2 + x = 28
x = 28−2 = 26 flowers

32. Alex,Gil and Meg shared 740 cards.Alex had 20 fewer cards than Gil and 120 fewer cards than Meg. How many cards did Alex have?


Alex's cards are 200 cards

Pembahasan

DIketahui:

Total of the cards = 720

Alex's cards = Gil's cards - 20

Alex's cards = Meg's cards - 120

Ditanya: Alex's card

Dijawab:

Alex's card = x

Gil's card = y

Meg's card = z

x = y - 20

y = x + 20

x = z - 120

z = x + 120

Total of the cards = x + y + z

740 = x + x + 20 + x + 120

740 = 3x + 140

3x = 740 - 140

3x = 600

x = 200

So, the cards that Alex have are 200

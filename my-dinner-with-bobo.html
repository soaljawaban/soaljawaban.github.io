PEMBAHASAN Simple Past Tense

Digunakan untuk menyatakan kejadian yang terjadi di masa lampau atau peristiwa yang ada di masa lalu.

Ket. waktu Past Tense:YesterdayLast NightLast week agoTwo days agoA few minutes ago

Rumus Past Tense(+) S + Verb 2 + O/C(-) S + did not/didn’t + Verb 1 + O/C(?) did + S + Verb 1 + O/C?

KESIMPULAN

1. yesterday evening they HAD dinner with Mr Jokowi

Alasannya:Verb-1 ➡️ HaveVerb-2 ➡️ Had

2. my cat DRANK chocolate milk two days ago

Verb-1 ➡️ DrinkVerb-2 ➡️ Drank

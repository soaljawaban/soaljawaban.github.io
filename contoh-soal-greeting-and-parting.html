21. contoh dialog ungkapan greeting, asking condition, asking personal info introducing , parting​


Jawaban:

Sure, here is an example of a dialog that includes expressions of greeting, asking condition, asking personal info, introducing, and parting:

* **Greeting**

```

A: Good morning, Mr. Smith.

B: Good morning, Ms. Jones. How are you today?

A: I'm doing well, thank you for asking. And you?

B: I'm doing well, too. Thank you for asking.

```

* **Asking condition**

A: How are you feeling today?

B: I'm feeling much better, thank you for asking. I was sick yesterday, but I'm feeling better today.

A: I'm glad to hear that.

* **Asking personal info**

```

A: What do you do for a living?

B: I'm a software engineer at Google.

A: That's interesting. What kind of software do you develop?

B: I develop web applications.

```

* **Introducing**

```

A: Mr. Smith, I'd like you to meet my colleague, Ms. Jones.

B: It's a pleasure to meet you, Ms. Jones.

A: The pleasure is mine, Mr. Smith.

```

* **Parting**

```

A: Well, I should probably get going. It was nice meeting you, Mr. Smith.

B: It was nice meeting you too, Ms. Jones.

A: I hope to see you again soon.

B: I hope so too.

```

I hope this helps!

37. What is the definition of greeting and parting?​


Jawaban:

The meaning of greeting is an expression that we use when you meet someone. The meaning of leave taking or parting is an expression that we use when we left someone.

Jawaban:

Greetings are a way for a person to communicate awareness of the presence of others, to show concern, and to emphasize or suggest the type of relationship or social status between individuals or groups of people who are related to one another. This is said when we just meet someone to greet him. For example, "Good morning", "Good afternoon", "Hello friends / Hello frend" etc.Parting is a sentence used to end a conversation, usually called a closing greeting. like goodbye, see you later, etc

39. rangkumlah materi ungkapan greeting, parting,Dan apologizing​


Greeting adalah ekspresi yang menunjukkan penyapaan, cara menyapa dan menyambut seseorang. Contoh: Good morning, Good night, etc.Parting adalah ekspresi yang menunjukkan perpisahan, berpisah dengan sesuatu/seseorang. Contoh: Bye, Goodbye, See you again, etc.Apologizing adalah ekspresi yang menunjukkan permintaan maaf, cara meminta maaf terhadap seseorang. Contoh: Sorry, I am sorry, etc.

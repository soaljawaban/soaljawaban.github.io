31. Soal bahasa inggris kelas 9 tolong yee...


Jawaban:

1)

Mr. Sulis has started fishing since he was 9 a.m.Mr. Sulis has started fishing for 4 hours.

2)

Cindy and Devi have begun practicing basketball since 6.30 a.m.Cindy and Devi have begun practicing basketball for three hours and forty-five minutes.

3)

Mrs. Anik has started working for our school since 2000.Mrs. Anik has started working for our school for nine years.

4)

Edo has joined the painting studio since he was 11.Edo has joined the painting studio for one year.

5)

Randy and his dad have started repairing his bicycle since 4 p.m.Randy and his dad have started repairing his bicycle for an hour.

Explanation

Present Perfect Tense adalah suatu pola kata kerja yang digunakan untuk menyatakan:

KEGIATAN YANG PERNAH TERJADI atau SUDAH TERJADI DARI DULU DAN BERLANJUT HINGGA SEKARANG. Misalnya:She has been a professional singer for three years. [Dia telah berprofesi sebagai penyanyi selama tiga tahun]He has been to Bali once. [Dia pernah ke Bali satu kali]

—

KEGIATAN YANG TELAH SELESAI SEKARANG. Misalnya:He has fixed your bicycle. Now you can ride it. [Dia sudah memperbaiki sepedamu. Sekarang kamu bisa naiki]They have made breakfast. [Mereka sudah menyiapkan sarapan]

Untuk membuat kalimat bentuk Present Perfect Tense, gunakan paduan rumus berikut.

Subject + has/have + Verb 3

dengan,

Subject =  Subjek kalimat.Have =  Kata kerja bantu untuk Present Perfect Tense.Verb 3 =  Kata kerja bentuk ketiga.

Catatan:

Has digunakan apabila subjek adalah: He, She, It, atau subjek tunggal.Have digunakan apabila subjek adalah: I, You, We, They, dan subjek jamak.

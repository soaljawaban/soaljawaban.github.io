30. the girls is a pretty (pretty,prettier,prettiest).. as her mother


Jawabannya Prettier !
Semoga benar dan membantu ✌️✌️✌️

Subject : English

Category : Degree of Comparison

Level : JHS VIII

Keyword : as ... as

Degree of Comparison atau perbandingan dalam bahasa Inggris ada 3 macam :

1. Positive degree = membandingkan dua benda yang kata sifatnya sama.

Pola : AS + adjective + AS

Contoh : I am AS TALL AS he is. (saya SAMA tingginya dengan dia)

2. Comparative degree = membandingkan dua benda di mana satu benda lebih daripada benda yang satunya lagi.

Pola : Comparative adjective + THAN

Contoh : I am TALLER THAN he is. (saya LEBIH tinggi daripada dia)

3. Superlative degree = membandingkan lebih dari dua benda di mana satu benda paling daripada yang lainnya.

Pola : THE + superlative adjective

Contoh : I am THE TALLEST in my family. (Saya PALING tinggi di keluarga saya)

ANSWER :

The girl is AS PRETTY AS her mother.

38. Have she doesn't on her face dimple


REARRANGE THESE JUMBLED WORDS INTO A GOOD SENTENCE

HAVE-SHE-DOESN'T-ON-HER-FACE-DIMPLE

___________________

JAWABAN :

She doesn't have dimple on her face .

{ Dia tidak mempunya lesung pipi di wajahnya }

Penjabaran :

She => Subyek dari pronoun "dia perempuan "

doesn't => Auxiliary verb negative form "tidak"

have => Action verb "mempunyai"

dimple => noun " lesung pipi "

on => preposition " di"

her => possessive adjective "kepemilikan nya ( perempuan )

face => noun "muka/wajah."

TIPS  :

~ Cermati arti masing-masing vocabulary

~ Tentukan subyek yang tepat dan logis.

~ Lihat struktur kalimat yang diterapkan ( meliputi tenses dan bentuk kalimatnya "positive form/negative form/interrogative form)

~ Terapkan pola penyusunan kalimat sederhana "SUBJECT+VERB+OBJECT/COMPLEMENT"

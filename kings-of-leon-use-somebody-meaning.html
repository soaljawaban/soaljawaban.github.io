A. Definition of Nonfiction Text Teks

Non-fiction text is a text that is made based on existing facts, non-fictional realities, or things that actually happen in life. Non-fiction texts use facts whose truth can be scientifically justified (objective).

Penjelasan:

Non-fiction texts are in the form of writings whose contents are not fictitious and are not the result of the author's imagination/design.

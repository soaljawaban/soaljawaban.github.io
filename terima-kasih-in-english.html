   Ketika kamu ingin menyampaikan sesuatu kepada kerumunan atau sekelompok orang, apakah yang akan kamu katakan pertama kali? Mungkin saja kamu akan meminta perhatian mereka. Kita menggunakan ungkapan untuk meminta perhatian ketika ingin menyampaikan sesuatu kepada orang.

   Menurutmu, bagaimanakah cara meminta perhatian menggunakan Bahasa Inggris? Apakah kamu akan mengatakan "Attention, please!"? Atau apakah kamu akan mengatakan hal lainnya? Nah, selain "Attention, please!" kita juga dapat menggunakan ungkapan-ungkapan berikut ini untuk meminta perhatian orang.

Pay attention, please! [Mohon perhatikan!]May I have your attention, please? [Bisa tolong perhatikan?]Excuse me! [Permisi!]Look! [Dengar!]Look at this! [Lihat ini!]Look at me! [Perhatikan aku!]Look what I've got! [Lihat apa yang aku dapatkan!]Hey! [Hai!]Hello, everyone! May I have the attention, please? [Halo semuanya! Boleh minta perhatiannya?]Can I have your attention, please? [Bisa minta perhatiannya?]Sorry to bother you. [Maaf mengganggu]Sorry for bothering you. [Maaf sudah menggangu]

   Untuk membalas ungkapan untuk meminta perhatian, kita dapat menggunakan:

Yes, please! [Ya, silahkan!]A'ight! [Baiklah!]Alright! [Baiklah!]What's that? Tell me about it! [Apa itu? Katakan pada 'ku!]Yes, sir! [Iya, pak!]Yes, ma'am! [Iya, bu!]Really? [Sungguh?]Okay! [Baiklah!]

Simple Present Tense

Nomor 1.

(+) My Sister and I cook in the kitchen together(-) My Sister and I DO NOT COOK in the kitchen together. (?) DO My Sister and I COOK in the kitchen together?

➡ (My Sister and I) boleh di ganti jadi 'we' ✔️

➡ Dan 'My Sister and I' juga boleh ✔️

Nomor 2.

(+) Sheril GETS a rank every year(-) Sheril doesn't get a rank every year(?) DOES Sheril GET a rank every year?

Nomor 3.

(+) I study english every night(-) I DO NOT STUDY english every night(?) DO I STUDY english every night?

➡ 'Do not' boleh disingkat jadi 'Don't' ✔

➡ 'Does not' boleh disingkat jadi 'Doesn't' ✔

PEMBAHASAN

Simple Present Tense adalah bentuk kata kerja dalam Bahasa Inggris yang berfungsi untuk menyatakan Kegiatan yg dilakukan secara berulang, rutin, kebiasaan dan hal2 bersifat umum/fakta.

Rumus Simple Present

(+) S + V1 (-s/es) + O/C(-) S + do/does + not + V1 + O/C(?) do/does + S + V1 + O/C?

Adverb of time

Every day Every year Every night

Catatan!

➡ Untuk nomor 2 karena Subjek tunggal maka:

kalimat positive menggunakan Verb+s/eskalimat negative dan interrogative menggunakan does

➡ Untuk nomor 1 dan 3 karena Subjek we / I maka:

kalimat positive menyatakan Verb1 tanpa tambahan -s/es️kalimat negative dan interrogative menggunakan do

Daily Routines

Daily routines adalah kegiatan yang dilakukan sehari-hari. Kegiatan daily routines ini sering juga disebut sebagai kebiasaan/ habit.

Saat kita ingin menyatakan kegiatan sehari-hari/ daily routines, kita perlu menggunakan Simple Present Tense. Simple Present Tense adalah tenses yang digunakan untuk menyatakan kejadian yang berlangsung sehari-hari (Daily routines).

Kata keterangan yang sering digunakan dalam simple present tense adalah:

• Every day

• Every week

• every month

• every year

• once a week

• twice a day

• three times a month

• three times a year

• always

• often

• usually

• seldom

• rarely

• dan lain lain

Pattern yang digunakan dalam simple present tense adalah:

(+) S + V1 atau S + V1s/es

(-) S + do/ does + V1

(?) Do/ Does + S + V1?

NB:

V1 dan Do untuk subject I, You, They, We

V1s/es dan Does untuk subject He, She, It

Pembahasan

Merubah kalimat positif ke negatif dan introgatif

1. (+) We go to Malang

(?) Do we go to Malang?

(-) We don't go to Malang

2. (+) My father reads newspaper

(?) Does your father read newspaper?

(-) My father doesn't read newspaper.

3. (+) I sing an English  

(?) Do you speak English?

(-) I don't speak English.

4. (+) They have breakfast every morning

(?) Do they have breakfast every morning?

(-) They don't have breakfast every morning.

5. (+) You watch TV every afternoon

(?) Do you watch TV every afternoon?

(-) You don't watch TV every afternoon

6.(+) the sun shines in the east  

(?) Does the sun shine in the east?

(-) The sun doesn't shine in the east.

7.(+) Rida goes to Surabaya every month

(?) Does Rida go to Surabaya every month?

(-) Rida doesn't go to Surabaya every month

8.(+) My mother sells mangoes in the market

(?) Does your mother sell mangoes in the market?

(-) My mother doesn't sell mangoes in the market

9(+) He rides his motorcycles to school every day

(?) Does he ride his motorcycles to school every day?

(-) He doesn't ride motorcycles to school everyday.

10.(+) I help my mother every morning

(?) Do you help your mother every morning?

(-) I don't help my mother every morning.

Jawaban:

||ACTIVE VOICE → PASSIVE VOICE||

1. I buy five english package books in the bookstore.

Passive → Five English package books are bought by me in the bookstore.

2. My mother is cooking soto betawi in the kitchen.

Passive → Soto Betawi is being cooked by my mother in the kitchen.

3. Mrs. Yeny taught english in muga junior high school.

Passive → English was taught by Mrs. Yeny in Muga Junior High School.

4. Rafi has washed his bicycle in the yard

Passive → His bicycle has been washed by Rafi in the yard.

5. Mr. Robby will take three balls in the basket.

Passive → Three balls will be taken by Mr. Robby in the basket.

Penjelasan:

Merubah kalimat active ke kalimat passive

||Langkah-langkah:||

Identifikasi terlebih dahulu kalimatnya, termasuk tense apakah kalimat tersebut.Apakah simple present tense, simple past tense atau tense lainnya.Jika sudah diketahui tense-nya, barulah kita dapat merubah kalimat tersebut ke dalam kalimat passive, dengan memperhatikan pola/rumus/formula dari tense tersebut.Ingat!Posisi subject dan object pada kalimat active dan kalimat passive itu berbeda.Subject pada kalimat active merupakan object pada kalimat passive, sedangkan object pada kalimat active merupakan subject pada kalimat passive.

||Formula/rumus:||

1. Simple Present Tense

Aktif : S + do/does + V1

Pasif : S + to be (am, are, is) + V3 + by + O

Contoh:

Aktif : She writes a letterPasif : A letter is written by her

2.Present Continuous Tense

Aktif : S + to be (am, are, is) + V1-ing + O

Pasif : S + to be (am, are, is) + being + V3 + by + O

Contoh :

Aktif : She is writing a letterPasif : A letter is being written by her

3. Present Perfect Tense

Aktif : S + have/has + V3 + O

Pasif : S + have/has + been + V3 + by + O

Contoh :

Aktif : She has written a letterPasif : A letter had been written by her

4. Simple Past Tense

Aktif : S + V2 + O

Pasif : S + to be (was, were) + V3 + by + O

Contoh :

Aktif : She wrote a letterPasif : A letter was written by her

5. Past Continuous Tense

Aktif : S + to be (was, were) + V1-ing + O

Pasif : S + to be (was, were) + being + V3 + by + O

Contoh :

Aktif : She was writing a letterPasif : A letter was being written by her

6. Past Perfect Tense

Aktif : S + had not + V3 + O

Pasif : S + had + been + V3 + by + O

Contoh :

Aktif : She had written a letterPasif : A letter had been written by her

7. Simple Future Tense

Aktif : S + will + V1 + O

Pasif : S + will be + V3 + by + O

Contoh :

Aktif : She will write a letterPasif : A letter will be written by her

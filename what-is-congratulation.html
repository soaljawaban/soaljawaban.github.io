37. what is the difference between congratulation and hope


Congratulation is the word you use to congratulate someone for their achievement. In example, they just won a championship so you say "Hey! Congratulations on your win!"

Hope is the word you use for your own satisfaction. In example, you're hoping for the rain to fall tonight so you say "I hope tonight will rain."

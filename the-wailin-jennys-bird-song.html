22. (+) the bird is on the cage(-)(?)​


- The bird is not on the cage

? Is the bird on the cage?

Soal:

(+) the bird is on the cage

(-)

(?)​Jawaban:

(+) The bird is on the cage.

(-)  The bird is not on the cage.

(?)​ Is the bird on the cage?

Penjelasan:

Present simple tense kalimat nominal:

Rumus:

(+) Subject + to be-1 + non verb + object + complement + adverb pf place + adverb of timer.

(-) Subject + to be-1 + not + non verb + object + complement + adverb pf place + adverb of timer.

(?) To be-1 + subject + non verb + object + complement + adverb pf place + adverb of timer +question mark.

Aplikasi:

(+) Subject (The bird) + to be-1 (on the cage) + non verb + object + complement + adverb of place (on the cage) + adverb of time.

(-) Subject  (The bird) + to be-1 (is) + not (not) + non verb + object + complement + adverb pf place (on the cage) + adverb of timer.

(?) To be-1 (Is) + subject (the bird)+ non verb + object + complement + adverb pf place (on the cage) + adverb of timer +question mark (?)

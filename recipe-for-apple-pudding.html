2. Ingredients NOVEL APPLE PUDDING​


To make an apple pudding, the ingredients typically include:

1. Apples: Choose fresh and ripe apples that are suitable for baking or cooking. The specific variety can vary based on personal preference or availability.

2. Sugar: Used to sweeten the pudding and enhance the flavor of the apples. The amount of sugar can be adjusted according to taste.

3. Butter: Adds richness and flavor to the pudding. It helps to create a smooth and creamy texture.

4. Cinnamon: A warm and aromatic spice that complements the flavor of apples. It adds a delightful depth to the pudding.

5. Lemon juice: Used to prevent the apples from browning and also adds a tangy flavor that balances the sweetness.

6. Breadcrumbs or bread slices: These are used as a base or filling for the pudding. It provides texture and helps bind the ingredients together.

Optional ingredients:

- Raisins: Can be added for additional sweetness and texture.

- Nutmeg: A warm and earthy spice that pairs well with apples.

- Vanilla extract: Adds a hint of sweetness and enhances the overall flavor.

- Whipped cream or ice cream: Can be served as a topping to complement the pudding.

Please note that the specific recipe and measurements may vary depending on the recipe you choose to follow.

36. apa artinya Novel apple pudding


Jawaban:

Arti dari Novel Apple Pudding yaitu

Ingredients (bahan-bahan)

4 green apples, peeled, cored and thickly sliced (4 apel hijau, kupas, buang biji dan diiris tebal )

¼ cup/45 g self-raising flour, sifted (¼ gelas / 45 g tepung terigu siap saji, diayak )

60 g butter or margarine, cubed (60 g mentega atau margarin, potong dadu)

½ cup/100g caster sugar (½ cangkir / 100 g gula kastor atau gula halus)

1¼ cups/300ml water (1¼ gelas / 300ml air)

Methods (cara pembuatan)

1. Place apple in a heat-proof dish. Place flour in a bowl. Rub in margarine using fingertips.  (Tempatkan apel di piring tahan panas. Tempatkan tepung di mangkuk. Oleskan margarin menggunakan ujung jari.)

2. Stir in sugar and water (mixture should be lumpy). Pour over apples.  (Aduk gula dan air (campuran harus kental). Tuangkan apel.)

3. Bake at 180°C for 1 hour or until syrupy and golden. Serve warm with cream or ice cream.  ( Panggang pada suhu 180°C selama 1 jam atau hingga menjadi seperti sirup dan berwarna keemasan. Sajikan hangat dengan krim atau es krim.)

Serves 6  (Porsi 6)

Preparation time 15 minutes  (Waktu persiapan 15 menit)

Cooking time 60 to 70 minutes (Waktu memasak 60 hingga 70 menit)

Penjelasan:

Self-raising flour adalah tepung terigu yang di dalamnya telah terdapat bahan pengembang dan ragam. Jadi, tepung terigu ini tidak perlu ditambahkan bahan pengembang seperti baking soda, dan garam.

Caster sugar adalah gula yang sangat halus. Biasanya berbentuk bubuk.

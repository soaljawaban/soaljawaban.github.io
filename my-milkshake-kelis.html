16. apa manfaat milkshake mangga dan keistimewaanya​


Milkshake mangga adalah minuman yang populer yang terbuat dari campuran mangga, susu, dan es krim atau yogurt. Berikut adalah beberapa manfaat dan keistimewaan dari milkshake mangga:

  Manfaat:

Kaya Nutrisi: Mangga adalah sumber vitamin C, vitamin A, serat, dan antioksidan. Susu juga memberikan protein dan kalsium. Jadi, milkshake mangga dapat memberikan nutrisi penting untuk tubuh.

Energi Tambahan: Milkshake mangga yang mengandung susu dan gula dapat memberikan tambahan energi, membuatnya cocok sebagai camilan atau sarapan yang mengenyangkan.

Pilihan Makanan Sehat: Dengan bahan-bahan yang alami seperti mangga dan susu, milkshake mangga bisa menjadi alternatif makanan sehat dibandingkan dengan camilan yang lebih tidak sehat.

  Keistimewaan:

Rasa yang Lezat: Mangga memberikan rasa manis dan segar pada milkshake, membuatnya enak diminum. Rasa manis mangga bisa menggoda lidah siapa pun.

Variasi dan Kreativitas: Anda dapat menambahkan berbagai bahan tambahan ke dalam milkshake mangga, seperti es krim, yogurt, madu, atau biji chia, untuk menyesuaikan rasa dan tekstur sesuai selera Anda.

Penyegaran di Cuaca Panas: Milkshake mangga adalah minuman yang sangat menyegarkan terutama di cuaca panas. Dengan es batu yang ditambahkan, ini adalah minuman yang ideal untuk meredakan haus dan membuat tubuh Anda merasa dingin dan nyaman.

Kepopuleran Global: Milkshake mangga adalah minuman yang sangat populer di banyak bagian dunia, sehingga mudah ditemukan dan dinikmati di berbagai tempat.

Kecocokan dengan Menu Makanan: Milkshake mangga dapat menjadi pendamping yang sempurna untuk hidangan penutup atau makanan ringan lainnya, seperti pancake, wafel, atau kue.

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Meskipun milkshake mangga memiliki manfaat dan keistimewaan, perlu diingat bahwa jika terlalu banyak gula dan kalori tambahan ditambahkan, ini dapat menjadi minuman tinggi gula dan tinggi kalori. Oleh karena itu, penting untuk mengonsumsinya dengan bijak dan memperhatikan jumlah gula dan kalori yang ada dalam resep.


17. make a procedure text about "how to make a milkshake


Materials=

- 4 cups vanilla ice cream
- 2 cups milk
- 1 tbs chocolate sauce (optional)
- 1 cup fresh strawberries

Steps=
-Place milkshake glasses into the freezer. Using cold glasses will help the shake stay cool to give you time to enjoy. Leave for up to an hour if possible.-Soften ice cream. It is easier to make a shake when the ice cream is slightly soft. Take the ice cream out of the freezer a few minutes before making your shake.-Place the ingredients into a blender. Mix the ice cream, milk and added flavors into the blender. Blend on high for one minute. Don’t leave in the blender for longer or the shake will melt quickly.-Pour shakes into frosty glasses. Take the glasses out of the freezer as soon as the blending is complete. Then pour the shake mixture in-Drop a straw into the shake, then enjoy. You can sip a shake right from the glass but somehow the straw adds to the flavor and the experience.
Ingridients
4 cups vanilla ice cream
2 cups milk
4 teaspoons chocolate sauce (optional)
1 cup fresh or frozen strawberries

Steps
1. Place milkshake glasses into the freezer. Using cold glasses will help the shake stay cool to give you time to enjoy. Leave for up to an hour if possible.

2. Soften ice cream. It is easier to make a shake when the ice cream is slightly soft. Take the ice cream out of the freezer a few minutes before making your shake.

3. Place the ingredients into a blender. Mix the ice cream, milk and added flavors into the blender. Blend on high for one minute. Don’t leave in the blender for longer or the shake will melt quickly

4. Pour shakes into frosty glasses. Take the glasses out of the freezer as soon as the blending is complete. Then pour the shake mixture in.

5. Drop a straw into the shake, then enjoy. You can sip a shake right from the glass but somehow the straw adds to the flavor and the experience.

18. bagaimana cara membuat milkshake ke bahasa inggris pakai cara,alat,bahan tolong jawab ya?


You’ll need:

1/3 cup milk. You can use 2%, whole milk, or a blend of milk and half-and-half.

1-1/2 cups ice cream (vanilla makes a good base for most shakes, or you can use a flavor like chocolate)

Delicious mix-ins! Think a swirl of chocolate syrup, peanut butter, a chopped banana or other fruit, a handful of chocolate or butterscotch chips (we could go on and on…)

A blender, of course. You can use a classic stand blender-or an immersion blender if you want to make it right in your glass.

step ;

1. Put your glass in the freezer to get a nice chill on it.

2. Make sure your ice cream is tempered, soft but not runny. This step is key -- if the ice cream is too hard, you'll add too much milk and thin it out, diluting the concentration of the ice cream. You can also use frozen yogurt and low-fat ice cream.

3. Take three hearty scoops of your favorite ice cream, add two ounces of milk and any flavorings you want (chocolate syrup, candy or cookie pieces). Do not add ice as it will just water it down. If you like your milkshakes thinner, add more milk.

4. If you're adding cookies to your milkshake, Brett recommends adding three roughly crushed cookies to the ice cream.

5. Top it with whipped cream and additional cookies/candy if you desire.

29. Make a procedure text about "how to make a milkshake"


Ingridients
4 cups vanilla ice cream
2 cups milk
4 teaspoons chocolate sauce (optional)
1 cup fresh or frozen strawberries

Steps
1. Place milkshake glasses into the freezer. Using cold glasses will help the shake stay cool to give you time to enjoy. Leave for up to an hour if possible.

2. Soften ice cream. It is easier to make a shake when the ice cream is slightly soft. Take the ice cream out of the freezer a few minutes before making your shake.

3. Place the ingredients into a blender. Mix the ice cream, milk and added flavors into the blender. Blend on high for one minute. Don’t leave in the blender for longer or the shake will melt quickly

4. Pour shakes into frosty glasses. Take the glasses out of the freezer as soon as the blending is complete. Then pour the shake mixture in.

5. Drop a straw into the shake, then enjoy. You can sip a shake right from the glass but somehow the straw adds to the flavor and the experience. How To Make A Milkshake

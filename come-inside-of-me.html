S+will/shall+v1 + if + S+ do not/does not + v1

MAPEL : BAHASA INGGRIS

MATERI : CONDITIONAL SENTENCE


SOAL DAN PEMBAHASAN

==========================

The rain will leak inside if the roofer_____(not,come) soon.

the rain will leak inside if the roofer (does not,come) soon

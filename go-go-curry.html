7. prosedur teks how to make white chicken curry


Jawaban:

At first, I wanted to make grilled chicken, but I was annoyed that the chicken chef cut a lot of it. And because yesterday I finished learning to cook curry from my mother-in-law. Try the mother-in-law's chicken curry menu with simple ingredients.

Ingredients :

1/2 kg chicken (cut into 6 bags)

1 lime

ground spices:

5 cloves of red onion

3 cloves of garlic

3 big red chilies

1 finger turmeric

1 slice ginger

1 tbsp coriander powder

2 pecans

other seasonings:

1 lemongrass in geprek

1 slice of galangal

1 bay leaf

2 lime leaves

salt and sugar

oil

Step :

1. Wash the chicken with lime then wash it clean.

2. Saute the ground spices with other spices, stir-fry until it turns a darker color and the smell comes out.

3. Add chicken, stir

4. Add water little by little until the chicken is cool

5. Add sugar salt

6. Cook until cooked

7. Serve warm

14. Minta resep nya cara buat curry dong


￼

Cookpad untuk Android

     (54.099)

Pasang

￼

Daftar 

Ngobrol seputar resep Curry Rice khas Restoran Jepang Praktis dan Enak!...

 

 

￼

Curry Rice khas Restoran Jepang Praktis dan Enak!

 4

Sudah lama sekali pengen masak curry rice, dan akhirnya kesampean! ...selengkapnya

￼ Ekitchen

Ikuti

Bahan-bahan

4 porsi

100 gram Bumbu curry rice merek golden curry (mild)

2 buah Kentang ukuran sedang

1 buah Wortel ukuran besar

1/2 buah bawang bombay ukuran sedang

2 sendok makan Minyak untuk menumis

500 ml Air kaldu ayam

secukupnya Air untuk merebus

Langkah

30 menit

￼

Siapkan bumbu kari ini (saya ketemu di carefour) saya pilih yang mild, kalau di tokonya sih ada yang pedas.

￼

Kupas dan cuci bersih wortel dan kentang.

￼

Potong wortel di tengah dalam keadaan horizontal (---) lalu potong lagi di tengah dalam keadaan vertikal (|) supaya lebih kecil, dan potong bentuk kotak.

￼

Potong kentang sesuai selera

￼

Siapkan panci, dan masukan wortel, kentang dan air lalu rebus selama 15 menit (bisa juga dikukus sekitar 15-20menit).

￼

Potong bawang bombay, cara potong bawang bombay bisa dilihat dari resep Bernadette --> http://dapurmasak.com/resep/how-to-dice-an-onion-6895

Tumis bawang bombay dengan 2 sdm minyak hingga harum. lalu masukan bumbu kari semuanya.

￼

Aduk hingga rata dan tambahkan air sedikit demi sedikit hingga mencapai kekentalan yang kamu inginkan. Semakin banyak air semakin cair.

￼

Aduk terus supaya tidak gosong di bawah dan masukan wortel dan kentang (saya goreng dulu kentangnya), dan tambahkan merica

Sajikan bersama nasi PANAS Atau disajikan bersana dengan ayam garing khas taiwan minus bumbu (bisa dilihat di --> http://dapurmasak.com/resep/ayam-garing-pedas-ala-taiwan-6752)

24. prosedur teks how to make white chicken curry


Jawaban:

INGREDIENTS

1 Small chicken, cut into pieces

2 cups fresh coconut milk (or 200 ml thick coconut milk and 200 ml water)

GRINDED SPICES

Shallots, garlic, galangal, lemongrass, cumin, salt, pepper, coriander

COOKING DIRECTIONS

Boil chicken. Allow 2 cups of broth to remain in the pot.

Fry the grinded spices with 2 spoons of vegetable oil.

Add the spices into the boiled chicken; turn to low heat until it is tender.

Add the coconut milk, stir, continue simmer to enhance flavor.

Serve your white chicken curry while hot.

33. Tolong bikinin presentasi tentang makanan curry memakai bahasa inggris! (Yang agak panjang)


Chicken curry is a common delicacy in South Asia, Southeast Asia, as well as in the Caribbean (where it is usually referred to as "curry chicken"). A typical South Asian curry consists of chicken stewed in an onion and tomato-based sauce, flavored with ginger, garlic, mango chutney, tomato puree, chili peppers and a variety of spices, often including turmeric, cumin, coriander, cinnamon, cardamom and so on. Outside South Asia, chicken curry is often made with a pre-made spice mixture known as curry powder.

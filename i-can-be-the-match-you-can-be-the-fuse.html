There are many ways for you to make requests in English politely. Most of the polite requests are in the form of questions.

Would you / Would you like to / Could you …?

The most common polite way is to use Would you/Would you like to/Could you to ask other people to do somethings. We use it to suggest or request something more polite than Do you want to …?

Polite requests Answer Yes Answer No

Would you please give me the file on the table? Yes, of course. Well, I am afraid …

Could you join us at a party on this Sunday? Yes, I am happy to join I am sorry I am busy this Sunday.

Would you like to join us at a party on Sunday? Yes, certainly I’d like to but … + reason.

Would you mind / Do you mind …?

Would you mind + Verb-ing

Do you mind if I + Verb

If you think the answer maybe negative, and you want to sound more polite, you can use Would you mind …?

Would you mind helping with my exercise?

Yes, certainly.

When you leave the room, would you mind closing the door?

No, not at all!

We use Would you mind if I or Do you mind if I to make a request, we may be anticipating possible objections:

Would you mind if I take your car to work today?

No, not at all.

Do you mind if I go out to buy some apples now?

No, of course not.

Remember that ‘Do you mind…?’ and ‘Would you mind…?’ mean ‘Is it a problem for you?’ so the polite answer when we ‘say yes’ is ‘No’.

Asking for permission

Can I / Could I / May I / Might I

We use Can I / Could I / May I / Might I to request something for your self, all of these forms are possible. May and Might are consider to be more polite than Can and Could. We see the following examples:

Can I have a biscuit?

Yes, of course

Could I ask you a favour?

Of course you can.

Could I possibly have another sandwich?

I don’t think so. You’ve had too much.

Might I leave the class a bit earlier today?

Yes, you can.

If you’ve finished with the computer, may I borrow it?

Yes, please do.

Might is more frequenly used in indirect questions, as an indirect softens the request. Note the further polite alternatives that we can use.

I wonder if I might leave the class a bit earlier?

Others way to say it are:

Would it be OK if I left the class a bit earlier?

Would I be able to leave the class a bit earlier?

[section title=”Remember”] It’s more polite to use these phrases.

Would you mind if I…?

Could I possibly…?

Could you possibly…?

Do you think you could…?

[/section]

Pembahasan

Congratulations/to congratulate adalah ekspresi untuk memberikan ucapan selamat kepada orang lain atas prestasi atau pencapaian mereka.

1. Untuk mengucapkan selamat kita bisa memakai ekspresi congratulations atau bentuk kata kerja dasarnya adalah to congratulate. Jadi, kita bisa langsung mencari kedua kata kunci tersebut di dalam dialog.

Yang perlu digarisbawahi adalah to congratulate. Meskipun Kakak bilang kata kuncinya adalah to congratulate, kita gak bisa nih hanya tulis to congratulate you on winning the second place. Karena ini adalah bentuk kata kerja dasar, 'kalimat' tersebut belum lengkap. Untuk jadi kalimat yang lengkap, ingat yaa rumusnya subject + verb + object. Jawabannya jadi i would like to congratulate you on winning the second place of the taekwondo competition! Yang digarisbawahi di akhir juga harus ikut ditulis karena dia menjelaskan prestasi yang dicapai.

2. Di nomor ini kita diminta untuk memberitahu achievement (pencapaian) yang Rifath dapatkan. Mengacu pada dialog Yoga yang pertama, Yoga congratulate Rifath on winning the second place of the taekwondo competition.

Untuk reading comprehension, kita bisa menuangkan jawaban dengan mengulang kata-kata yang ada di pertanyaannya. Misalnya pada pertanyaan ini, kalimatnya memakai verba to get (mendapatkan). Jadi, kita bisa menjawab pertanyaan dengan verba yang sama menjadi Rifath gets the second place of the taekwondo competition.

3. Kalau melihat dialog 6, Rifath hanya mengatakan selamat. Untuk itu, kita juga harus memerhatikan dialog Rifath yang sebelumnya untuk mengetahui pencapaian Yoga yang diberi selamat. 4. Untuk pertanyaan ini, kata kuncinya ada di verba hope. Di dalam dialog, verba tersebut muncul di bagian akhir dialog 8.  Seperti yang Kak Qya katakan sebelumnya, kita bisa langsung menjawab pertanyaan sesuai kalimat yang tertuang dalam dialog sehingga jawaban nomor 4 adalah Rifath hopes Yoga can be a great author in the future. 5. Di pertanyaan kamu, Kak Qya gak melihat ada kata yang digarisbawahi. Karena Yoga punya banyak pengalaman terbaik, pertanyaannya minta kita untuk menjelaskan satu pengalaman spesifik yang Yoga maksud. Berdasarkan dialog Rifath yang sebelumnya memberi selamat atas publikasi puisi Yoga, dapat kita ketahui bahwa pengalaman inilah yang sedang dibicarakan. Untuk itu, jawabannya adalah It refers to the publication of Yoga's poem by the Creative Magazine.

Kata publication (publikasi) itu sendiri merupakan kata benda/nomina dari verba to publish. Kata tersebut memang gak muncul secara langsung di dalam dialog, tetapi terkadang hanya dari verbanya kita bisa menebak nominanya kalau memang belum tahu pasti. Selain menebak, tentunya kita bisa memperkaya kosa kata dengan cara banyak belajar dan banyak membaca, intinya memperbanyak pengalaman untuk membiasakan diri. Kalau berdasarkan kata-kata yang muncul di dalam dialog, kamu bisa mereformulasi kalimat Kak Qya menjadi It refers to Yoga's poem being published by the Creative Magazine.

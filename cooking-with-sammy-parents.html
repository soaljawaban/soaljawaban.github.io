4. (+) (-)Rahma did not learn cooking with her mother (?)​


Jawaban:

(+) Rahma did learn cooking with her mother

(?) Did Rahma learn cooking with her mother?

Jawaban:

Simple Past Tense

(+)S+Verb(2)+O

(-)S+Did+not+Verb(1)+O

(?)Did+S+Verb(1)+O?

→(+)Rahma learned cooking with her mother.

→(-)Rahma did not learn cooking with her mother

→(?)Did Rahma learn cooking with her mother?

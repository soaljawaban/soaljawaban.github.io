22. + My mother cooks in the kitchen yesterday.- My mother didn't cook in the kitchen yesterday.? Did my mother cooks in the kitchen yesterday?Find the mistakes.​


Kalimat + salah

kalimat ? juga salah

bentuk past tidak perlu menambahkan s/es setelah verb

yang benar:

+) my mother cooked

?) did my mother cook

Jawaban: COOKS

Yang ditanyakan Find the mistakes (temukan kesalahan) ?

(+) My mother cooks in the kitchen yesterday. ❌

(-) My mother didn't cook in the kitchen yesterday.✅

(?) Did my mother cooks in the kitchen yesterday?❌

Penjelasan:

Berdasarkan kalimat pada soal terdapat kata YESTERDAY (kemarin) maka kalimat tersebut adalah PAST TENSE yaitu kejadian masa lampau.

Kalimat yg SEHARUSNYA Berdasarkan Rumus Past Tense:

(+) S + VERB 2 + O

= My mother COOKED in the kitchen yesterday. ✅

(-) S + DIDN’t/DID NOT + VERB 1 + O

= My mother didn't COOK + in the kitchen yesterday.✅

(?) DID + S + VERB 1 + O

= Did my mother COOK in the kitchen yesterday?✅

Catatan tentang PAST TENSE:

(+) pada kalimat Positive menggunakan VERB 2 = COOKED

(-) (?) tapi pada kalimat Negative dan Interrogative menggunakan VERB 1 = COOK karena sudah ada DID

JADI:

Yang salah pada SOAL adalah kata kerja/ VERB = COOKS.

36. (+) She is cooking in the kitchen(-) She ..?.. in the kitchen.​


Present Continuous

(+) She is cooking in the kitchen

(-) She is not cooking in the kitchen ✔️ (?) is she cooking in the kitchen? ✔️

PEMBAHASAN Present Continuous

Digunakan untuk menyatakan Kegiatan yg terjadi sekarang atau sedang berlangsung saat ini.

Rumus Present Continuous(+) S + is/am/are + Verb-ing(-) S + is/am/are + not + Verb-ing(?) is/am/are + S + Verb-ing?

Catatan

Subjek + Tobe

I + amIt/He/She + is ✔️You/we/they + are

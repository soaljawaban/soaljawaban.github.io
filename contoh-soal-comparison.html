29. pengertian contoh dan rumus comparison degrees​


degree comparison: tingkat perbandingan. memperbandingkan sifat 2 benda.

a. positive : Subject 1 + To be 1 + As + Adjective/ adverb + Subject 2

b. comparative : s + to be + adjective/adverb + er + Than + subject 2 ( untuk satu kata, jika dua kata tidak menggunakan er)

c. Super lative: most + adjective or adverb (of all) + est

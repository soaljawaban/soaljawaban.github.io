3. Apa perbedaan Congratulations Congratulations on to › dan Congratulations for​


Jawaban:

When congratulations is followed by a NOUN, then we always say congratulations ON

Example : Congratulations on the promotion

When congratulations is followed by a VERB in the -ING form, then we can say ON or FOR

Example : Congratulations for winning the Math competition

8. jelaskan pengertian dari1. congratulations on 2. congratulations for 3. congratulations to​


Jawaban:

1. CONGRATULATIONS ON, untuk peristiwa yang mebhagiakan.

• contoh : congratulations on your wedding

2. CONGRATULATIONS FOR, untuk orang yang memenangkan sesuatu, peristiwa/pencapaian.

• contoh : congratulations for your graduation

3. CONGRATULATIONS TO, digunakan langsung ke orang yang bersangkutan.

• contoh : congratulations to you, my brother.

Question:

"Hello everybody. I would like to introduce myself. My name is Byan."

The underlined expression is .....

a. Self-introducingb. Introducing someone to othersc. Greetingd. Leave-taking

Answer:

Greeting (c).

Jika kalimat tidak digaris bawahi, maka teks tsb merupakan teks perkenalan diri/self-introducing. Namun krn yg digaris bawahi hanya kalimat "Hello everybody." maka kalimat tsb merupakan kalimat sapaan/greeting (c).

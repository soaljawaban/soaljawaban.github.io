38. Dari teks 'holiday in semarang waterfall' Pertanyaan nya : Is semirang a beautiful waterfall?


Recount Text

Recount Text adalah teks yang menceritakan tentang kejadian atau pengalaman di masa lampau. Pengalaman tersebut bisa jadi pengalaman sendiri ataupun orang lain.

Generic Structure dari Recount Text adalah:

Orientation : It gives the readers the background information needed to understand the text, such as who was involved, where it happened, and when it happened. Events : A series of events, ordered in a chronological sequence. Re-orientation :A personal comment about the event or what happened in the end.

Language Features dari Recount Text adalah:

Using the simple past tense, past continuous tense, past perfect tense, and past perfect continuous tense. Using temporal sequence, e.g. On Saturday. On Monday, On Sunday Focus on specific participant, e.g. I (the writer) Using the conjunctions, such as: then, before, after, etc. Using action verd, e.g. went, stayedPembahasan

Holiday In Semarang

On Sunday, I and my best friend, Sari, visited Semirang Waterfall in Ungaran. It was the first time for me to visit the waterfall.

When we arrived at the hill, I felt so fresh and I could enjoy the scene. The air was so pure and all I could see only green and green.

In Ungaran, we took a little bit trekking to find Semirang Waterfall. It was too bad for me because the distance to see the waterfall was too far.

After taking so far distance, we found Semirang Waterfall. What a beautiful waterfall. We enjoyed the nice water in the rain forest surrounding the waterfall.

Hearing the sound of falling water made me peace and relax. It was also excellent drowning out background sound. Playing with the water made me feel so happy.  

Finally, the time was over. It was time for us to go home. It was an unforgettable moment. I really enjoyed it.

Question: Is semirang a beautiful waterfall?

Answer: Yes it is. In the paragraph 4, the writer said that Semirang is a beautiful waterfall. It is surrounded by rain forest.

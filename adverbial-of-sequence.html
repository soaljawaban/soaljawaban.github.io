7. the first term of arithmetic sequence is -4 and the 21th term is 116. 31th term of arithmetic sequence is...​


Jawaban:

176

Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

Use this formula :

a_n = a_1 + (n - 1) * d

In this case, you're given:

• The first term (a_1) = -4

• The 21st term (a_21) = 116

Calculate the common difference (d) using the information given for the 1st and 21st terms, then solve the d.

a_21 = a_1 + (21 - 1) * d

116 = -4 + 20d

20d = 116 + 4

20d = 120

d = 120 / 20

d = 6

Now you can find the 31st term (a_31):

a_31 = a_1 + (31 - 1) * d

a_31 = -4 + 30 * 6

a_31 = -4 + 180

a_31 = 176

So, the 31st term of the arithmetic sequence is 176.

16. materi pembahasan tentang adverb of sequence


1. Sequence adverbs often come at the start of a sentence or clause and act as a marker as to order.
2. However, some sequence adverbs like 'first' and 'next'  can come at the end of a sentence or clause.
3. 'Next', 'then' and 'after that' are interchangeable. They mark something between the start and finish.
4. We often use sequence markers when giving instructions on how to do something, make something or go somewhere.

example adverb of sequence:
First, I dropped my bag in a mud puddle.  Then, a car drove by and splashed water on my clothes.  Next, I tripped over my bag and I fell in the puddle.  After that, my glasses fell off.  Finally, I began to cry.

22. How do you reduce of adverbial clauses into adverbial phrases?


An adverb clause can be shortened to an adverb phrase. This can be particularly helpful when you want to express your ideas in a more concise manner. Before you reduce an adverb clause into an adverbial phrase, make sure that the adverb clause (subordinate clause) and the main clause have the same subject.

14. Ubah kalimat dibawah ini menjadi kalimat passive:1. I have bought a new car.2. My Sister has started a new job this week.3. She has already submitted her paper.4. My father has broken his leg.5. They have had a newborn baby.​


Jawaban:

1. A new car has been bought by me

2. A new job has been started by My sister this week

3. her paper has already been submitted by her

4. His leg has been broken by my father

5. a newborn baby has been had by them

PembahasanActive Voice adalah kalimat aktif yaitu Subject melakukan aksi/tindakan dalam kalimatnya Passive Voice adalah kalimat Pasif yaitu Object dikenai aksi/tindakan oleh Subjek dalam kalimatnya.

Berikut Berdasarkan Rumus Present Perfect

Active: S + has/have + V3 + OPassive: O + has/have + been + V3 + By Subject

Maka:

1. I have bought a new car.

A new car has been bought by me

2. My Sister has started a new job this week.

A new job has been started by My sister this week

3. She has already submitted her paper.

her paper has already been submitted by her

4. My father has broken his leg.

His leg has been broken by my father

5. They have had a newborn baby.

a newborn baby has been had by them

22. 5. There is a rare condition in babies that causes excessive internal bleeding, which can cause brain damage and even death. Newborn babies in most countries in the world are routinely given vitamin K either by injection or orally. Suggest how these two facts might be linked. COMENCE​


Pendarahan dalam artinya terjadi suatu masalah dimana darah tidak bisa membeku. Hal ini lah yang membuat pendarahan terjadi dan sulit dikendalikan. Pemberian vitamin K akan berdampak pada pendarahan tersebut dimana vitamin K sangat memiliki andil yang besar dalam proses pembekuan darah. Jadi, hubungan antara pendarahan dengan vitamin K yaitu vitamin K dapat mengatasi pendarahan yang terjadi.

Penjelasan:

Fungsi utama vitamin K yaitu membantu dalam proses pembekuan darah. Jika tubuh kekurangan vitamin K, maka darah akan sulit membeku. Hal ini mengakibatkan orang yang kekurangan vitamin K cenderung gampang mengalami perdarahan dan luka yang sulit sembuh/membaik.

Kekurangan vitamin K lebih pada umumnya dialami oleh bayi baru lahir. Pada orang dewasa, kekurangan vitamin K dapat disebabkan oleh beberapa kondisi, seperti mengkonsumsi obat pengencer darah, pola makan yang buruk, hingga gangguan penyerapan nutrisi atau malabsorsi.

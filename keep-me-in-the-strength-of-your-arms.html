13. Kalimat passive dari the doctor advised me "keep your health well"


Passive Voice.

Geberic structure Passive Voice adalah tobe + V3

Jadi Kalimat diatas adalah Kalimat Past Tense V2.

Caranya :
Gabungkan Generic Structure Keduanya

O/adv + Past + Passive Voice + by + S
V2 tobe +V3
V2 combine tobe : Was/were.
jadi structurenya berubah menjadi ;

O/Adv + Was/were + V3 + by + S

Active Voice :
The Doctor advised me "keep your health well'
S. V2. O. Phrases

Passive Voice :
I was advises by the doctor "Keep your health well"

S + was + V3 + by + O


Past : V2
Passive Voice : tbe + V3

In marketing strategy, first-mover advantage (FMA) is the competitive advantage gained by the initial ("first-moving") significant occupant of a market segment.

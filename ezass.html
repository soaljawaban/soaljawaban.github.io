Jawab:

[tex]K = 2\pi r\\= 2 \frac{22}{7} 7\\= 44\\\\44/2 = 22[/tex]

Lingkaran

jari jari = 7 m

------------------------------

K : 2 × π × r

: 2 × 22/7 × 7

: 44/7 × 7

: 44

: 44 ÷ 2

: 22

[tex]harga \: beli \: = 1.250.000 \\ \\ harga \: jual \\ (\frac{3}{5}\times 20 \times 125.000) + ( \frac{2}{5} \times 20 \times 70.000) \\ 1.500.000 + 560.000 = 2.060.000 \\ \\ \%untung = \frac{harga \: jual- harga \: beli}{harga \: beli} \times 100\% \\ \\ \%untung = \frac{2.060.000 - 1.250.000}{1.250.000} \times 100\% \\ \\ \%untung = \frac{810.000}{1.250.000} \times 100\% \\ \\ \%untung = 64.8\%[/tex]

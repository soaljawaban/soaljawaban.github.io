Jawaban: COOKS

Yang ditanyakan Find the mistakes (temukan kesalahan) ?

(+) My mother cooks in the kitchen yesterday. ❌

(-) My mother didn't cook in the kitchen yesterday.✅

(?) Did my mother cooks in the kitchen yesterday?❌

Penjelasan:

Berdasarkan kalimat pada soal terdapat kata YESTERDAY (kemarin) maka kalimat tersebut adalah PAST TENSE yaitu kejadian masa lampau.

Kalimat yg SEHARUSNYA Berdasarkan Rumus Past Tense:

(+) S + VERB 2 + O

= My mother COOKED in the kitchen yesterday. ✅

(-) S + DIDN’t/DID NOT + VERB 1 + O

= My mother didn't COOK + in the kitchen yesterday.✅

(?) DID + S + VERB 1 + O

= Did my mother COOK in the kitchen yesterday?✅

Catatan tentang PAST TENSE:

(+) pada kalimat Positive menggunakan VERB 2 = COOKED

(-) (?) tapi pada kalimat Negative dan Interrogative menggunakan VERB 1 = COOK karena sudah ada DID

JADI:

Yang salah pada SOAL adalah kata kerja/ VERB = COOKS.

15. (+) my mom ... many vegetables in the market yesterday(-) my mother ... many vegetables at the market yesterday(?) ... my mom buy many vegetables in the market yesterday?​


Halo

Simple Past tense adalah tense yang digunakan untuk menerangkan sesuatu yang sudah lampau.

Rumus Simple Past:

(+) S + V2 + O

(-) S + did not + V1 + O

(?) Did + S + V1 + O?

Jadi,

(+) My mom bought many vegetables in the market yesterday

(-) My mom didn't buy many vegetables at the market yesterday

(?) Did my mom buy many vegetables at the market yesterday?

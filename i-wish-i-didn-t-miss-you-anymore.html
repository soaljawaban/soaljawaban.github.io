8. APA ARTI I LOVE YOU , I MISS YOU,I WANT YOU


Arti dari I love you, I miss you, I want you adalah saya cinta kamu, saya merindukan kamu, saya inginkan kamu.

Pembahasan

Kalimat di atas sering digunakan untuk menyatakan cinta (love expressing). Pola yang digunakan adalah simple present tense, karena mengungkapkan suatu kebenaran atau suatu fakta.

Polanya adalah: Subject + v1 + object

Subject dari kalimat di atas adalah I (saya)

Predicate atau verb-nya adalah love (cinta), miss (rindu), want (ingin/mau)

Object-nya adalah you (kamu)

---------------------------

Detil Jawaban

Kelas: -

Mapel: Bahasa Inggris  

Bab: Expessings  

Kode: -

Kata Kunci:  love expressing, simple present tense

29. you didn`t eat my toast (?) (+)​


Jawaban

(-) You didn't eat my toast.

(+) You ate my toast.

(?) Did you eat my toast?

Penjelasan

Simple past tense digunakan untuk menyatakan kejadian masa lampau.

Formula:

(+) S + V2 + O

(-) S + did not + V1 + O

(?) Did + S + V1 + O + ?

Berikut contohnya:

(+) She played chess last night.

(-) She did not play chess last night.

(?) Did she play chess last night?

(+) They went to a party.

(-) They did not go to a party.

(?) Did they go to a party?

(+) He broke up with his girlfriend.

(-) He did not brake up with his girlfriend.

(?) Did he brake up with his girlfriend?

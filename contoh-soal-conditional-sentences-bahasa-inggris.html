If you need assistance, press here. = Jika Anda memerlukan bantuan, tekan di sini.
If you don’t come on time, you miss such a lot. = Jika Anda tidak datang tepat waktu, Anda kehilangan begitu banyak.
If you feel tired, it’s a good idea to go to bed. = Jika Anda merasa lelah, itu adalah ide yang baik untuk pergi tidur.
If it rains, it’s good for the garden. = Jika hujan, itu baik untuk taman.
If I eat too many sweets, I put on weight. = Jika aku makan permen terlalu banyak, aku meletakkan berat badan.
If I get up too early, I feel tired all day. = Jika aku bangun terlalu pagi, saya merasa lelah sepanjang hari.
If I have toothache, I go to the dentist’s. = Jika saya memiliki sakit gigi, saya pergi ke dokter gigi.
If I feel ill, I make an appointment with my GP. = Jika saya merasa sakit, aku membuat janji dengan GP saya.
If I read without my glasses on, I strain my eyes. = Jika saya membaca tanpa kacamata saya di, aku galur mataku.

14. 5 contoh kalimat future conditional sentence,present conditional sentence,and past conditional sentences


future/ type 3 :
If + subject + had + V3/past perfects + subject + would have + V3/past perfect
- If I had found her address, I would have sent her an invitation.
- If you had remembered to invite me, I would have attended your party.
- If he had asked you for forgiveness, would you have forgiven him?
- If he had called you, you could have gone.
- If I had had enough time, I would have come to your birthday party.

present/ type 1 :
If+ subject + VI (Simple Present Tense) + Subject + will + VI (Simple Present tense
- If I work hard, I will get a lot of money.
- If I have a lot of money, I will buy an expensive car.
- If I study hard, I will pass the final examination. 
- If I have free time, I will go swim at On The Rock Hotel.
- If I find her address, I’ll send her an invitation.

past/ type 2 :
If + subject + V2/simple past tense + Subject + would + Vl/past future tense
- If I were you, I would apply for that scholarship.
- If l got high a score in the final examination, my mother would give me sports car
- My husband would buy me a new car if he got a job in foreign company. 
- If I found her address, I would send her an invitation.
-  If I were you, I would not do this.

36. Contoh conditional sentences


Tipe pertama :

1. If it rains, I will stay at home

2. If I see Anton, I will call him

3. If Heru bring me the book, I will study hard

4. If he steal my wallet again, I will to defeat him

5. If I graduate this year, I will go to Malaysia for holiday

Tipe kedua :

1. I will call Anton, if I see him

2. I will stay at home, if it rains

3. I will study hard, if Heru bring me the book

4. I will to defeat him, if he steal my wallet again

5. I will go to Malaysia for holiday, if I graduate this year

Pokoknya yang tipe kedua ini kayak dibolak-balik aja dari yang tipe pertama ya

Tipe ketiga (ingat yang ini pakai past perfect tenses ya) :

39. Apa contoh type 1 conditional dalam bahasa inggris


Jawaban:

Conditional Sentence type 1 digunakan ketika kita ingin mengungkapkan sesuatu yang memiliki kemungkin akan terjadi di masa yang akan datang.

Rumusnya

If+simple present, simple future (will)

Contoh:

"If i come home early, i will get some rest"

"Jika aku pulang lebih awal, aku akan beristirahat"

Nah jadi maksudnya jika kamu pulang lebih awal, kemungkinan untuk bisa beristirahat itu ada dan bisa terjadi.

CONTOH

If it rains, you will get wet.

You will get wet if it rains.

If Sally is late again I will be mad.

I will be mad if Sally is late again.

If you don't hurry, you will miss the bus.

You will miss the bus if you don't hurry.

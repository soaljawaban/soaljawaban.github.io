19. Write sentences with the following gerunds 1. Complaining


Answer: 

Gerunds is a noun taken from a verb and added by -ing. Gerunds are usually preceded by adjectives or verbs. 
Examples of verbs that is preceding gerunds are
- like, love, hate, remember, forget, fancy
Examples of adjectives that is preceding gerunds are 
- keen on, interested in, bored with. 

Here's the sentences:
- I never like complaining about the service.
- She's keen on complaining about the motorcyclists on the street.

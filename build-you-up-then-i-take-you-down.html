Jawaban ini dijawab sesuai dengan lirik lagu yang sudah disediakan. Jawaban dijawab dengan mencari verb, adjective dan noun pada lirik lagu tersebut.

Pembahasan

Verb adalah kata yang mendefinisikan aksi, tindakan dan pengalaman. Yang termasuk verb dalam lirik lagu tersebut adalah Left, pour, feel, lie, get, exchange, broke, said, take back, coming, save, fall, built, pull it down, fled, scared, known, become, reach, could, go, wonder, leave, hurt, love.Adjective adalah kata yang digunakan untuk menerangkan kata benda. Yang termasuk adjective dalam lirik lagu tersebut adalah Hard, high, down, shaky.Noun adalah kata yang digunakan untuk menyebut orang, benda dan tempat. Yang termasuk noun dalam lrik lagu tersebut adalah Bird, tree, light, heart, head, Atlantis, town, ground, love.

2. To build up a thank you letter, one can follow these steps:

Begin with a formal salutation such as "Dear [Name],"Express your gratitude and mention specifically what you are thankful forReinforce your appreciation and how it has positively impacted you or your organizationEnd with a closing statement and your signature

3. The format of thank you letter that I make usually includes:

A clear heading and dateA formal salutation and opening sentence to express gratitudeMain body to explain the specific reason for the thank you, and how it impacted the writerClosing sentence and signatureContact information

4. Thank you letter is similar to a friendly letter but it is more formal and specific, it is used to express gratitude and appreciation towards a specific person or organization, while friendly letters are used for casual communication.

It helps in building and maintaining good relationships with people, whether it is a customer, a client, or an employee

It shows that you are an appreciative and considerate person, and it can also create a positive image of you or your organization

It can be used as a tool for networking and to keep in touch with people

In case of a job application, it can also be used to show that you are still interested in the position and the company.

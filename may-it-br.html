12. Ubahlah menjadi bentuk spoken=1. It is on October 12. It is on November 123. It is on May 204. It is on December 255. It is on May 5​


Jawaban:

If you put the day before the month:

1. It is on October 1 = It is on the first of October.

2. It is on November 12 = It is on the twelfth of November.

3. It is on May 20 = It is on the twentieth of May.

4. It is on December 25 = It is on the twenty fifth of December.

5. It is on May 5 = It is on the fifth of May

Penjelasan:

If you put the month before the day, use the definite article before the day in British English. In American English, the definite article can be dropped.

1. It is on October 1 = It is on October (the) first.

2. It is on November 12 = It is on November (the) the twelfth.

3. It is on May 20 = It is on May (the) twentieth.

4. It is on December 25 = It is on December (the) twenty fifth.

5. It is on May 5 = It is on May (the) fifth.

Pembahasan

Jawaban Reflecting Bab V Buku Bahasa Inggris Kelas 7

Situation 1

Lisa : Beni, do you have a pet?

Beni  : Yes, I do. I have a dog.

Lisa : I love dogs! What is it like?

Beni : It’s a he. He has curly brown fur and big round eyes. He is very playful.

Lisa  : Wow! I can imagine how adorable he is.


Situation 2

Siti  : Dayu, do you often go to the park near your house?

Dayu : Yes, I do.

Siti  : What is it like?

Dayu : It’s beautiful. There are so many beautiful flowers and big trees.

Siti  : That’s great! We should go there together some time.


Situation 3

Udin : Do you know Farhan Ramadhan?

Dayu : Yes, I do.

Udin : What is he like?

Dayu : He is kind and helpful.  


Situation 4

Lisa : Siti, you look so happy today.

Siti  : Yes, My mother bought me a new cell phone yesterday.

Lisa : May I see it?

Siti  : I want to focus on study at school, so I left it at home.  

Lisa  : What does it look like?

Siti  : It has big screen and the color is pink.

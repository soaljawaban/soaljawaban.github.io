36. 6. A mathematics Competition consist 40 multiple choice questions. 4 marks are awarded for a correct answer while -2 marks is deducted for a wrong answer. Negative 1 marks will be awarded or deducted for questions not attempted. The student get 32 correct answers and 6 wrong answers. Find the score's student obtained!​


Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

In this math competition, the student gets 4 points for each correct answer and loses 2 points for each wrong answer. If the student answers 32 questions correctly and 6 questions incorrectly, then the score obtained from the correct answers is 32 x 4 = 128 points, while the score deducted from the wrong answers is 6 x (-2) = -12 points.

Out of a total of 40 questions, the student answered 32 + 6 = 38 questions, so there are 40 - 38 = 2 questions that were not answered. For each unanswered question, the student will lose 1 point, so the score deducted from the unanswered questions is 2 x (-1) = -2 points.

So, the total score obtained by the student is 128 + (-12) + (-2) = 114 points.

2. buatlah karangan panjang b inggris tentang "a great country respects the heroes"!


RESPECT HEROES

Most of us know that building a country or nation is difficult. The foundation must be strong and the first builders were common people who wanted to see their land became great.

For instance, a country called Indonesia. According to historians, the archipelago country that is located at the equator had countless struggles. Starting from colonization from Portuguese to British, Indonesian people were crushed but not broken. They defend themselves by jungle warfare or guerilla to international meetings.

The defenders were the heroes. Great examples were Tjoet Njak Dhien from Aceh, General Soedirman, Soekarno, Hatta.

In conclusion, a great country or nation must respect their heroes. Without them, a country will be wiped out from the map.

3. Aji respects Alex because... attitude is very good. a. his b. her c. its d. their? ​


Jawaban:

Possessive Adjectives:

A. His

Penjelasan:

Jawaban dicetak tebal pilihan A. His dan merupakan materi pembelajaran Possessive Adjectives (kata sifat kepemilikan) dalam Bahasa Inggris.

Sikapnya Alex yang dimaksudkan oleh soal, maka kita memerlukan kata sifat kepemilikan sebagai jawaban, yaitu pilihan A. His

Kata "His" merujuk kepada sikapnya Alex yang membuat Aji menghormati (Respect) kepada Alex.

Pilihan lainnya salah.

1) Because

5)Becausecara mudah menjawab because atau because of adalah dengan melihat kalimat yang diberi konjungsi. bila ada subject-nya, maka because. bila tidak ada subject-nya, maka because of.

cara dengan rumus:
because diikuti dengan kalimat lengkap.
because of diikuti dengan noun phrase.

1. I turn on the light, BECAUSE it's getting dark outside (it : subject dan juga kalimat lengkap)
2. He doesn't like going to the beach, BECAUSE it's too hot there.
3. BECAUSE OF bad weather, the flight is delayed for 2 hours. (bad weather adalah noun phrase)
4. The world respects Einstein, .BECAUSE OF his knowledge. (his knowledge adalah noun phrase)
5. BECAUSE she wakes up late, she misses the bus. (she : subject dan berupa kalimat lengkap)

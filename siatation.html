28. contoh kalimat formal situation neutral situation informal situation


- informal; usually used in an informal situation with shortened form or slang words in it and done between friends
example:
- Dude, howdy? long time no see.                
- Do me a favour, would you? Just be invisible for a while.

- neutral: usually used in a formal or informal situation and normally uttered by someone who's just learned the language or to someone we barely know yet. 
example:
- How are you? It's been a long time since the last time I saw you.               
- Could you do me a favour? Just disappear for a while. 

- formal: usually used in a formal situation and normally uttered to someone respected.
example:
- How have you been, Mr.Lopez? It has been a while since the last time we met.

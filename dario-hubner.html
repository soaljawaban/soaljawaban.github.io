8. sejumlah gas yang berada dalam tabung berpiston ditekan pada suhu yang tetap sehingga volumenya 1/4 kali dario volume awalnya. jika tekanan gas mula-mula adalah 760mmHg maka tekanan luarnya adalah...


Diketahui :
[tex] P_{1}=760mmHg [/tex]
[tex] V_{2}= \frac{1}{4} V_{1} [/tex]
Ditanyakan [tex] P_{2} = ..... ? [/tex]
Jawab
Karena Pada Suhu tetap maka ini adalah proses isotermis/isotermik
Persamaan keadaan Isotermik [tex] P_{1} . V_{1} = P_{2} . V_{2} [/tex]
maka :
[tex] P_{2} = \frac{P_{1} . V_{1}}{V_{2}} [/tex]
[tex] P_{2} = \frac{760 . V_{1}}{ \frac{1}{4} V_{2}} [/tex]
[tex] P_{2} = 760 . 4 [/tex]
[tex] P_{2} = 3040 mmHg [/tex]
[tex] P_{2} = 304 cmHg [/tex]

Terdapat 3 kalimat Simple Past Continuous Tense pada dialog di atas, yakni:

Were you looking for a certain book? (verb) Yes, I was looking for a historical novel (verb) Where were you yesterday? (nominal) I was reading at the corner (verb) There was a cupboard covering me (nominal)_______________________

Pembahasan!Simple Past Continuous Tense

Fungsi: Menyatakan suatu kegiatan yang sedang berlangsung di masa lalu

Pattern (Verb)

(+) S + was/were + V-ing + O(–) S + was/were + not + V-ing + O(?) Was/Were + S + V-ing + O + ?

Pattern (Nominal)

(+) S + was/were + adj/n(–) S + was/were + not + adj/n(?) Was/Were + S + adj/n

23. ka tolong buatkan 10 dialog kayak gini ya.. tolong banget ka ​


PART = MAKING A CONVERSATION -  LIFE EXPERIENCE - PRESENT PERFECT TENSE - SIMPLE PAST TENSE

-------------

Jawaban:

Dee  : Have you ever visited  Bali, Sara ?

Sara : Yes, i have.  

Dee  : When did you go there?

Sara : I visited there two weeks ago

Dee  : Did you enjoy it?

Sara  : Yes, i did.

Dee  : Why did you choose Bali as your destination ?

Sara   : i did it because there was an specific event in Tanah lot temple then. Besides,   Bali has unique culture and beautiful beaches. I love it .

Dee   : What places did you visit ?

Sara :  I visited  eight  incredible places, such as Kuta beach , Uluwatu beach, Tanah Lot temple , Bali Botanic garden” Bedugul “, Ubud art market   , Besakih temple , Agung Rai Museum of art, and Puri Lukisan Museum

Dee  : How long did you stay there, Sara?

Sara :  I stayed there for six days.

Dee  :  If i have a long holiday, i will go there next month!

Sara : Certainly, You won’t regret, Dee!  

Note :

I apply three tenses in the conversation, among others : present perfect, simple past and simple present.

We use present perfect tense " Have you ever visited ?"  to reveal the experience or the activity we did in the past and it is still related to the present (now).

We use simple past tense " i visited.....i stayed .....there was......" to reveal the activity we  finished in the past.

How to form the interrogative of simple past  by using Wh-Question, please take a look at the pattern below :

(+ ) Wh-word +  did + subject + verb 1 + object/complement?    ( verbal )

( - ) Wh-word + didn't + subject+ verb 1 + object /complement?   ( verbal )

( + ) Wh-word+ was/were+ subject+ noun/adjective/adverb.........?   ( nominal)

( - ) Wh-word+ wasn't/weren't+subject+noun/adjective/adverb......?(nominal )

We use simple present tense "Bali has unique culture and beautiful beaches. I love it " to reveal the general truth .

EXPLANATION :

SIMPLE PAST TENSESimple past tense mendeskripsikan tindakan yang telah dimulai dan diselesaikan juga pada waktu tertentu di masa lampau . Durasi waktu kegiatan tidak ditentukan, bisa saja baru - baru ini telah terjadi dan  kejadian yang memang sudah lama terjadi di masa lampau.

Berdasarkan struktur kalimatnya, dibagi 2 jenis yaitu verbal sentence & Nominal sentence .

1. Verbal sentence

merupakan struktur kalimat yang predikatnya berupa kata kerja aksi. Contoh : eat, run, drink, do, walk, read, watch, swim and etc.

Pattern of Verbal Sentence

(+) Subject + Verb 2 + Object/Complement  

(-) Subject + did + not + verb 1 + Object/ Complement

(?) Did+Subject+Verb 1 + Object/Complement ?

2.Nominal sentence

memiliki struktur kalimat dimana predikatnya tidak terdapat kata kerja aksinya. Pada umumnya diikuti kata benda (noun), kata sifat (adjective) dan keterangan ( adverb ).

Pattern of nominal sentence :

(+) Subject + tobe ( was/were ) + noun/adjective/adverb

(-) Subject + tobe(was/were) + not + noun/adjective/adverb  

(?) tobe(was/were)+Subject + noun/adjective/adverb?

ADVERB OF TIME

( Keterangan waktu identifikasi bentuk simple past)

1. Definite point in time ( waktu penentu jelas )

last week, yesterday, two years ago, last night, this morning

Example :

Verbal

(+ ) She watched a horror movie last night

( - ) She did not watch a horror movie  last night

( ? )Did she watch a  horror movie last night ?

Nominal

(+ ) We were in Paris  two years ago

( - ) We  were not in Paris two years ago

(? ) Were we in Paris two years ago  ?

2. An indefinite point in time ( dalam waktu yang tidak ditentukan )

a long time ago, the other day

Example :

Verbal  

(+ ) He lived in  the orphanage  when he was young.    

(- )  He did not live in the orphanage  when i was young.

( ? ) Did he live in the orphanage when i was young?

Nominal

(+) They were mercenaries a long time ago      

(- ) They were not mercenaries a  long time ago

( ? ) Were they  mercenaries a long time ago ?

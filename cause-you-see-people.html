6. What do you know about cause-effect text?


Jawaban:

Cause and effect is a common way to organize information in a text. Paragraphs structured as cause and effect explain reasons why something happened or the effects of something. These paragraphs can be ordered as causes and effects or as effects and then causes. The cause and effect text structure is generally used in expository and persuasive writing modes.

38. 1. John stayed up late, so John was tired in the morning.Cause: ..........Effect: ...........2. If you study hard, you will get better grades.Cause: ...........Effect: ............​


Penjelasan:

Cause (sebab) merupakan penyebab mengapa sesuatu dapat terjadi.

Effect (akibat) adalah hasil dari apa yang telah terjadi.

Cause and Effect sangat berkaitan satu sama lain. "If there is an effect, there must be a cause. If there is a cause, that could lead to effects."

Dalam membuat kalimat cause and effect, diperlukan conjunction (kata hubung), seperti: because, since, as, so, for, therefore, because of, due to, thus.

Contoh:

He was angry because we were late.

It is raining heavily outside, so I can't come to your party.

Since you can speak English, you have many friends from other countries.

Tasya can't go to school today for she is unwell.

The boss was happy as John did the work very well.

The school is under construction, therefore I don't go to school today.

Because of heavy rain, there were many car accidents last night.

5. what are the language feature whitch used in offering sentence.​


Jawaban:

feature Expression of offering

Penjelasan:

example

Could I give you a hand?

The phrase a hand here should not be taken literally. We're not really offering our hands, Squad. The phrase a hand is used metaphorically for a help. So, the meaning of this phrase is "may / can I help you?"

May I help you?

A phrase meaning "can I help you?" This is used when those who say they want to offer assistance to their interlocutors regarding things they feel they need help with.

Can I do anything to help?

This expression is used when the person who says it wants to offer help in whatever matter the person who said it could do to the other person.

May I offer my assistance?

This expression is usually used when someone says he wants to offer help by teaching the other person how to do something.

What shall I do for you?

This expression means "What shall I do for you?" In this expression, the person who said it offered his help by first wanting to know what he could do to help. The use of the word shall here is usually used in a more personal way, for people who are more closely related to the person who said it.

Would you like some help?

The phrase some help in this expression indicates that the person saying it is willing to provide some help that the other person might need.

Shall I help you with ...?

This expression is used when someone who says he wants to offer help with a certain thing that the other person is working on.

1. An.......pronoun is used to replace a person or thing that is acted upon of verb in a sentence (subjective / objective)

JAWABAN: OBJECTIVE

Penjelasan:

An objective pronoun acts as the object of a sentence—it receives the action of the verb. The objective pronouns are her, him, it, me, them, us, and you.

       - Cousin Eldred gave me a trombone

       - Take a picture of him, not us!

Object Pronouns adalah kata ganti milik (orang atau benda) yang berfungsi sebagai “object”. Dengan kata lain “kata ganti milik yang dikenai tindakan”.

Subjective Pronouns act as the subject of a sentence—it performs the action of the verb. The subjective pronouns are he, I, it, she, they, we, and you.

        - He spends ages looking out the window.

        - After lunch, she and I went to the planetarium.

Subjective pronoun adalah kata ganti yang menggantikan kata /frasa benda yang merupakan subjek sebuah kalimat.

28. decide whether the prepotional pharases in the sentence are used as adjectives or adverbs​


Jawaban:

Decide whether the prepositional phrases in the sentences are used as adjectives or adverbs.

a. The river outside the boundary is dangerous to cross.

(Sungai di sebelah luar perbatasan itu berbahaya untuk disebrangi)

ANSWER: Prepositional phrase as adjective.

'outside the boundary' tells which river is dangerous to cross. 'Di sebelah luar perbatasan' menyatakan sungai mana yang berbahaya untuk disebrangi. Maka, prepositional phrase ini berfungsi sebagai kata sifat (adjective).

b. All the passengers aboard the bullet train were frightened when they heard the explosion.

(Semua penumpang yang naik kereta peluru merasa takut ketika mereka mendengar ledakan)

ANSWER: Prepositional phrase as adjective.

'aboard the bullet train' tells which passengers were frightened when they heard explosion. 'yang naik kereta peluru' menyatakan penumpang takut ketika mereka mendengar ledakan. Maka, prepositional phrase ini berfungsi sebagai kata sifat (adjective).

c. My shopping list needs to be put into my purse, so that I won't forget it.

(Daftar belanjaku perlu dimasukkan ke dalam dompetku, sehingga I aku tidak melupakannya)

ANSWER: Prepositional phrase as adverb.

'into my purse' tells where the shopping list needs to be put. 'ke dalam dompetku' menyatakan tempat di mana daftar belanja harus diletakkan. Maka, prepositional phrase ini berfungsi sebagai kata keterangan (adverb).

d. We will order pizza during the break.

(Kami akan memesan pizza selama jam istirahat)

ANSWER: Prepositional phrase as adverb.

'during the break' tells when we will order the pizza. 'selama jam istirahat' menyatakan waktu kami akan memesan pizza. Maka, prepositional phrase ini berfungsi sebagai kata keterangan (adverb).

Penjelasan:

Prepositional Phrases

Prepositional Phrases adalah frasa yang menggunakan preposisi. Frasa ini dapat berfungsi sebagai kata sifat (adjectives) dan sebagai kata keterangan (adverbs).

Prepositional Phrase as adjectives memodifikasi kata benda atau kata ganti yang menjelaskan 'which', 'what kind', 'how much/many'. Prepositional Phrase as adjectives mengikuti kata benda atau kata ganti.

Contoh:

The computer in that room is broken. (which)

Is the book on the table yours? (which)

People at railway station are waiting for the train. (which)

The tree in front of my house is dying. (which)

Prepositional Phrase as adverbs memodifikasi kata kerja, kata sifat, atau kata keterangan yang menjelaskan 'where', 'when', 'how', or 'why'. Prepositional Phrase as adverbs dapat diletakkan di mana saja tanpa mengubah maknanya.

Contoh:

I will go to bookstore on Sunday. (when)

I put all your belonging in your room. (where)

I was at home when the accident happened. (where)

The event was cancelled because of heavy rain. (why)

Pembahasan

Disposable (line 8) in the text can be used correctly in the following sentence.

Untuk mengartikan kalimat diatas kita harus melihat inti bahasan dalam kalimat tersebut.

Disposable merupakan inti pembahasan yang memiliki arti :

Jika merupakan bentuk kata benda (Noun) maka artinya Benda sekali pakai. Jika merupakan bentuk kata sifat ( Adjective) maka dapat diartikan sifat dari suatu objek yang hanya dapat dipakai sekali kemudian dibuang (contoh : Disposable plastic bags [kantong plastik sekali pakai]) dan bisa juga berarti dapat digunakan secara gratis ( contoh : We got disposable book for everyone [ Kita mendapatkan buku gratis untuk semua orang]

jadi, kemungkinan arti dari keseluruhan kalimat 'Disposable (line 8) in the text can be used correctly in the following sentence' pada soal adalah :

"Benda sekali pakai (pada baris 8) dalam teks dapat digunakan pada kalimat berikut ini."

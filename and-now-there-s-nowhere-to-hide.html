PembahasanHow to use washing machine

1. Clothes

3. Washing machine

Steps/method:

1. Add your dirty clothes and detergent to the drum of the machine.

2. Choose the right washing speed/cycle

3. Set the water temperature, preferably cool.

4. Press the ON button.

5. Take off all the washed clothes from the machine once it’s done.


How to play hide and seek

Material:

1. Players/Children

2. Space for hiding


Steps/method:

1. Choose one player to close his/her eyes for several seconds, usually counting to 100. This player called seeker.

2. Other players hide themselves as soon as possible.

3. Seeker opens his/her eyes and try to find other players.

4. The first player found will be the next seeker, and the last to be found is the winner.

Are the children playing hide and seek in the garden now?

Kalimat di atas termasuk kalimat present continuous tense. Mengapa? karena adverb of time yang dipakai adalah "now" (sekarang)

present continuous tense itu tense yang digunakan untuk menyatakan kejadian atau peristiwa di waktu itu terjadi

adverb of time yang biasa dipakai:

-right now

-now

-at this time

-at the moment

Selanjutnya, mengapa verbnya "playing" ?

karena rumus present continuous tense itu

s + to be + verb1 ing + keterangan waktu (opsional)

Kalimat di soal itu interrogative sentece, jadi diubah saja letaknya, maka menjadi

to be + s + verb 1 ing + keterangan waktu (opsional) + ?

Are the children playing hide and seek in the garden now?

di soal subjek nya menurutku aneh, karena "they children" mungkin seharusnya "the children" atau "their children"

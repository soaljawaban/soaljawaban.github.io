Complete the following dialogues with suitable clauses/ sentences showing causes or effect.

6. Yasmin: How is the result of the math test?  

Raffa: The test was difficult and the time was also limited. Consequently, I got a bad mark.

7. Dona: We can't continue walking. It is raining heavily.

Lesty: The base camp is also still a long way from her, so we will rest for a while.

8. Icha: Sinyal internet cafe is always crowded.

Nanda: You're right. People are fond of browsing the internet there thanks to Sinyal internet cafe.

9. Brandon: Ouch ... It is very painful.!

Nilam: You have a big wound. You have to cover it because it is a serious condition.

10. Fariz: The students will have a flag hoisting ceremony. Therefore, they gather in the field.

Bella: Yes. The ceremony will begin in a few minutes.

​

Pembahasan

Cause (sebab) merupakan penyebab mengapa sesuatu dapat terjadi.

Effect (akibat) adalah hasil dari apa yang telah terjadi.

Cause dan Effect sangat berkaitan satu sama lain. "If there is an effect, there must be a cause. If there is a cause, that could lead to effects."

Dalam membuat kalimat cause and effect, diperlukan conjunction (kata hubung), seperti: because, since, as, so, for, therefore, consequently.

Contoh:

He was angry because we were late.It is raining heavily outside, so I can't come to your party.Since you can speak English, you have many friends from other countries.Tasya can't go to school today for she is unwell.The boss was happy as John did the work very well.The school is under construction, therefore I don't go to school today.

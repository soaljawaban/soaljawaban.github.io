8. arti kata landing adalah​


Mendarat atau dalam bahasa Inggrisnya Landing adalah bagian terakhir dari suatu penerbangan, di mana suatu penerbangan pesawat terbang kembali ke landasan. Pesawat terbang pada umumnya mendarat di suatu bandar udara diatas landasan pacu atau helikopter yang mendarat di helipad.

Di dalam KBBI dan PUEBI (Pedoman Umum Ejaan Bahasa Indonesia) arti kata landing adalah Pendaratan

*TRAIN COLLISION*
Train collision is the encounter of two trains inadvertently and caused the train to tumble or get out of the railroad tracks.

There are many factors that cause the collision of the train. It usually happens due to miscommunication, like when a moving train meets another train on the same line so that a collision can not be avoided. The train wheels will be out of the railway. Train collisions also occur because there is no latch on some train tracks. it become one of the causes of the collision of the train.

Train collisions sometimes occur because of Human Error because there are some machinists who don't implement the standard operating procedures established or due to the occurrence of setting a poor work schedule resulting in physical fatigue. Because of physical fatigue, Some Machinists are sleepy and even fall asleep when they drive the trains.

EXPRESSION AGREEMENT/DISAGREEMENT

Dalam kehidupan sehari-hari kita menggunakan expression opinion yaitu ekspresi untuk menyatakan pendapat/opini kita terhadap sesuatu hal.

Sebagai reaksi terhadap expression opinion adalah agreement atau disagreement.

Phrase yang sering digunakan dalam ekspresi ini adalah .

Expression Agreement

I totally agree with you.That's a good idea.I am totally with you.Yes, you right.I support your idea

Expression Disagreement

I don't think so.I'm afraid I'm disagree with you.That's not always true.I'd say the exact opposite.No. I'm not sure about that.

Expression Giving Opinion

In my opinionI believe (that)I am certain (that)I am strongly believeI am sureI think..

Setelah kita belajar mengenai ekpresi-ekspresi diatas, mari kita masuk dalam pembahasan soal yang diberikan.

Pembahasan

On a debate, a speaker said that the next general election will cause social crash.  You do not agree with the opinion. What do you say?

Answer: I don't agree with your opinion. The government, media and social media should help to give political education to the people and help to build a fair and free election,  so they can trust the government who runs the election. With a free and fair election, we can avoid the social crash.

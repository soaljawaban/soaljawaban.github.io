13. someone has just paid a compliment on your necktie


Someone has just paid a compliment on your necktie.

Compliment expression:

"What a nice tie! It really suits with your jacket."

"You look great with that necktie!"

Pembahasan

Expressions of compliments is an expression that we use to praise someone for their achievement, appearance, and/or to praise their belongings.

Pada soal "Someone has just paid a compliment on your necktie", tampak rancu artinya, karena 'seseorang baru saja membayar sebuah pujian untuk dasimu'. Jadi ada dua kemungkinan jawaban untuk soal ini, yaitu memberikan compliment (pujian) atau menanggapi pujian atas dasimu.

Tanggapan atas pujian (Compliment responding) di atas adalah

"Thank you"

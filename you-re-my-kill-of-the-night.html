5. write question. use correct form of the verb.1. you/watch/the film/last night ​


Jawaban:

(+) You watched the film last night.

(?) Did you watch the film last night?

atau

(?) What did you watch last night?

Penjelasan:

Pola kalimat di atas adalah Simple Past Tense, yaitu pola kalimat yang digunakan untuk kejadian diwaktu lampau.

(+) Subject + Verb 2 + Object

(-) Subject + did not + Verb 1 + Object

(?) Did + Subject + Verb 1 + Object

Pada kalimat positif ini, terdapat adverb of time (kata keterangan waktu) yaitu last night (tadi malam). Hal itu menandakan pola kalimat adalah lampau atau telah terjadi.

7. My cat didn't kill the bird Ubah ke kalimat pasif


MAPEL : BAHASA INGGRIS

MATERI : ACTIVE AND PASSIVE VOICE.


SOAL DAN PEMBAHASAN

========================

My cat didn't kill the bird. (Active simple past)


Rumus simple past

(+) Subject + V2 + Object

(-) Subject + Did + Not + V1 + Object

(?) Did + Subject+ V1 + Object


Karena kalimat di atas merupakan kalimat negatif, maka berikut adalah rumus kalimat pasive dalam bentuk negatif.

(-) Subject + To be (was/were) + not + V3 + By...


CATATAN !!!

Subject kalimat aktif berubah jadi object kalimat pasif.


THE ANSWER

============

My cat didn't kill the bird… (active)

Passive nya :

=> The bird was not killed by my cat.

The bird was not killed by my cat

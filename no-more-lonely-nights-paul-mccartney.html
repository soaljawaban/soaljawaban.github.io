1. apakah paul mccartney anggota grup band the beatles


Ya, Paul McCartey adalah anggota dari group band The Beatles.

Pembahasan  

The Beatles adalah sebuah kelompok pemusik yang berasal dari wilayah Inggris dan memiliki sebuah aliran Rock. Band The Beatles dibuat di wilayah liverpool pada tahun 1960 yang kemudian akan sering untuk dianggap sebagai sebuah pemusik dan memiliki kesuksesan terbesar secara komersial dan juga menjadi sebuah hal yang dimana akan paling banyak untuk mendapatkan berbagai macam bentuk pujian ke dalam bagian musik populer. Kemudian, The Beatles terbentuk atas empat macam orang anggota. Yaitu John Lennon yang bermain gitar ritem dan vokal, Paul McCartney yang diman akan bermain gitar bass serta vokal, George Harrison yang akan bermain gitar utama maupun vokal dan terkahir adalah Ringgo Starr yang akan menjadi drum maupun vokal.

3. paul would be a good artist if he …….... more patience


Jawaban:

paul would be a good artist if he had more patience

Jawaban:

Paul would be a good artist if he were more patience

Penjelasan:

Jawaban dicetak tebal dan merupakan materi pembelajaran bentuk kalimat Conditional Sentence Type 2 (kalimat pengandaian tipe 2) karena pada kenyataannya, Paul tidak menjadi seniman yang bagus karena dia tidak sabar.

Kalimat pengandaian tipe 2

Fakta: Kondisi yang tidak mungkin terwujud di masa kini.

If + subject + V2/simple past tense + Subject + would + Vl/past future tense

atau

If + subject + were + adjective/noun+Subject + would be + adjective/noun

Contoh:

If l were you, I would accept that job offer. Fakta: I am not you. (aku bukanlah dirimu)

36. no more caption needed artinya?


No more caption artinya tidak diperlukan lagi adanya penjelasan atau pejelasan tambahan tidak diperlukan.

Pembahasan : Sufficiency and Excess

Sufficiency is a condition that what someone need is fulfilled. Excess is a condition that what someone need is more than then fulfilled.

Sufficiency

Subject + To be + Adj./Adv. + enough + to + verb

Example :

That motorcycle is old enough to rideThat kids is tall enough to be the basketball athlete She is beautiful enough to be the actress

Subject + have/has+ enough + noun + to + verb + adverb

Example :

I have enough money to buy that shirts She has enough hand sanitize to use by her brotherGoogle have enough space to save your data Excess

Subject + To be + too + Adj + to + verb

Example :

Meira is too young to be an actress Sam is too short to be the soldier This t-shirt is too small to use

Subject + Verb + too + Adj. + for ... + to + verb

Example :

The robber runs too fast for the police to chase Sam fix the transmission cable that too dangerous for the young man to do How we can Express Our Sufficiency to Reject Someone Offer

Human is live in social conditions that it is a possibility if someone offer me anythings they have, but in other perception, we have same thing that he/she have. So we can reject it with the polite word ? How can we do it ? Looks at the text :

Budi : "Hi Andi, I have some bread. Let's we eat it together !"

Andi : "Oh yes, thank you very much Budi, but uhm I'm sorry, I eated a lot of meatball portion an our ago"

Budi : "This okay Andi, have a nice day"

Andi : "Yeah sure, have a nice day too"

On the text we see that Andi is rejected the Budi offer because he eated a lot of meatball portion. Andi reject it with the polite word so Budi not feel angry about that.

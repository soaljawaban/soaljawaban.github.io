Karena Daren mengatakan "Selamat pagi Mrs Wijaya" maka, Mrs Wijaya membalas dengan mengatakan "Selamat pagi juga Daren" atau dalam bahasa Inggris "Good morning too Daren.

Offering help adalah ungkapan menawarkan bantuan kepada orang lain.

Complete these dialogue with expressions to offer help and the responses

1.Mrs lina's first daugter: What can I do for you, Mom?

Mrs lina:The Floor is dirty,is needs sweeping

Mrs lina's first daugter: Okay Mom.

Mrs lina: You're such a nice daughter. Thanks you

2.Mrs lina's second daugter: No problem Mom.

Mrs lina:your bed room is so mess.You need tidyng up your room.

Mrs lina's second daugter: Yes, Mom. I will tidy up soon.

Mrs lina: That's very kind of you. Thanks you

3.Mrs lina's third daugter: Could I help you, Mom?

Mrs lina:Your clothes pile up on the floor.Would you like to pack your clothes.

Mrs lina's third daugter: Sure, Mom.

Mrs lina: That's great.Thanks.

Pembahasan

Contoh kalimat offering help:

May I help you?Can I help you?What can I do for you?Would you like me to .. ?Would you like something to ..?Do you want me to join us?

Response:

AcceptingYes, please!Yes, of course!That's very kind of you.That would be very nice.Thanks. I would love to

Refusing No, thanks!I can't ...That would be marvelous but ... I'd love to, but I don't  ...No, I really won't thank you.

1. The distance from city a to city b on a map is 5 cm. the scale on the map is 1 : 780,000. the real distance from city a to city b is . . . . 19 km 29 km 49 km 39 km


Jawaban:d 39 km

Penjelasan:

//menghitung luasan jarak sebenarnya

5 cm x 780.000 = 3.900.000 cm

//konversi cm ke km

3.900.000 : 100.000 =. 39 Km

-

Jawaban :

39 km  

Penjelasan :

→ Diketahui :

Jarak Pada Peta ( JP ) = 5 cm Skala ( S ) = 1 : 780.000

→ Ditanya :

Jarak Sebenarnya  ( JS ) = ????

→ Penyelesaian :

JS = JP × S                        

= 5 × 780.000                    

= 3.900.000  cm

Ubah ke satuan km

JS =  3.900.000 × 1 / 100.000                    

= 3.900.000 / 100.000                    

= 39 km

So, the real distance from city a to city b is 39 km

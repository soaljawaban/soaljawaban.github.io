9. 1. "Stand against the wall" ! she told us.2."clean up the room,hilda",He said3.I said "Don't go near the snake!"(ubah ke dalam reported command)​


REPORTED SPEECH

1. She ordered us to stand against the wall.

2. He ordered Hilda to clean up the room.

3. I forbade to go near the snake.

EXPLANATION

Reported speech/indirect speech adalah kalimat berita; pernyataan ulang dari apa yang telah disampaikan orang lain.

Hal² yang diperhatikan dalam merubah direct speech ke reported speech :

1) tidak menggunakan tanda petik ganda

2) perhatikan jenis kata pengiringnya [reporting verb]. Jika menggunakan bentuk present [says, has said, will say], noun clause verb tidak mengalami perubahan tenses.

Sebaliknya, jika berbentuk past [said, told, asked, warned], noun clause verb mengalami backshift.

3) perhatikan jenis kalimatnya, apakah termasuk afirmasi, tanya dan imperatif [saran, nasihat, larangan, perintah].

4) sudut pandang pewarta [point of view]

BASED ON YOUR QUESTIONS

(1)

"Stand against the wall" ! she told us.

➡ She ordered us to stand against the wall.

(2)

"Clean up the room, Hilda", he said

➡ He ordered Hilda to clean up the room.

(3) I said, "Don't go near the snake!"

➡ I warned not to go near the snake.

or

➡ I forbade to go near the snake.

Note!

Dari ketiga kalimat langsung yang ditanyakan atas, semuanya berupa kalimat imperatif, jadi noun clause verb kalimat tak langsungnya berupa infinitive. Kata pengiring disesuaikan konteks kalimatnya.

11. 23. My mother told me, "Don't open the windownow!"a. My mother told me not to open thewindow at that timeb. My mother told me don't open thewindow at that timec. My mother told me didn't open thewindow at that timed. My mother told me not to open thewindow nowe. My mother told me not open the windownow​


REPORTED SPEECH

A. My mother told me not to open the

window at that time

EXPLANATION

Indirect speech (reported speech) merupakan pelaporan ulang ulasan yang disampaikan oleh narasumber, tanpa mengubah makna tersirat dalam pemberitaan pada tempo waktu yang berbeda.

Perubahan tenses pada noun clause verb-nya bergantung pada bentuk kata kerja pelaporannya { reporting verb } dan jenis informasi yg disampaikan.

Jika reporting verb-nya berbentuk lampau { said, told, asked }, noun clause verb mengalami backshift :

Simple present=>Simple pastSimple past=>Past perfectPresent perfect=>Past perfectSimple future =>Present conditionalPresent continuous=>Past continuousPast continuous=>Past perfect continuous

Ini dikecualikan jika pernyataan yang disampaikan merupakan informasi faktual, tidak mengalami backshift meskipun reporting verb-nya bentuk lampau.

Example :

1. Mrs. Dew said to me, " I am going to go to Singapore next week."

➡ Mrs. Dew said to me she was going to go to Singapore the following week.

2. Mrs. Dew said to her daughter " Don't be late to school."

➡ Mrs. Dew said to her daughter not to be late to school.

3. Mrs. Dew warned me, "Don't climb mount Bromo now! It's rainy."

➡ Mrs. Dew warned me not to climb mount Bromo at that time. It was rainy.

ELABORATION

My mother told me, "Don't open the window now!"

Don't open the window now!"↪Prohibition

↪Negative infinitive ( not+to+base verb )

↪Now >>> at that time/then

➡ My mother told me not to open the window at that time

S = 4(T - 12)

T + 48 = 4T - 48

48 + 48 = 4T - T

96 = 3T

T = 96/3

= 32

S = 32 + 48

= 80

19. Stacey baked 48 more cupcakes than Tammy.After Tammy ate 12 cupcakes, Stacey had 4 times as many cupcakes as Tammy. How many cupcakes did Stacey have?


Jawaban:

80 cupcakes

Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

Let's use algebra to solve the problem:

Let x be the number of cupcakes that Tammy baked.

Stacey baked 48 more cupcakes than Tammy, so Stacey baked x + 48 cupcakes.

After Tammy ate 12 cupcakes, she had x - 12 cupcakes left.

After Stacey ate no cupcakes, she had x + 48 cupcakes left.

Stacey had 4 times as many cupcakes as Tammy, so:

x + 48 = 4(x - 12)

x + 48 = 4x - 48

48 + 48 = 4x - x

96 = 3x

x = 32

Therefore, Tammy baked 32 cupcakes and Stacey baked 32 + 48 = 80 cupcakes.

After Tammy ate 12 cupcakes, she had 20 cupcakes left.

After Stacey ate no cupcakes, she had 80 cupcakes left, which is 4 times as many as Tammy's 20 cupcakes.

So the final answer is: Stacey had 80 cupcakes.

28. beda nya fall in love sama fell in love apa ya? contonh penggunaannya?makasi ya sebelumnya ​


Perbedaan dari "fall in love dan fell in love" adalah...

Jawaban :

=> Meskipun kedua nya memiliki arti yang sama yaitu "Jatuh cinta" akan tetapi dalam penggunaannya kalimat tersebut sangat berbeda.

1). Fall in love => Bentuk present tense (masa sekarang)
Rumus : S + V1 (s/es) + O
Contoh : I fall in love with you today. (Saya jatuh cinta dengan mu hari ini)

2). Fell in love => Bentuk past tense (masa lampau)
Rumus : S + V2 + O
Contoh : I fell in love with you yesterday. (Saya jatuh cinta dengan mu kemarin)

Fall => V1
Fell => V2
Fallen => V3

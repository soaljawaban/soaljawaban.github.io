25. What do you know about gerund? Explain it completly!


gerund adalah kata benda yang dibentuk dengan menambahkan akhiran -ing pada kata kerja
contoh: read+ing=readingAnswer:
_______
As far As I know, "Gerund" is a word that can be formed from the verb.
How to form the verb? How to add the suffix -ing so that it serves as a noun. This gerund can be a subject, direct object, or complementary subject in a sentence.

example:

Swimming is my best hobby
=> Swimming is a gerund that functions as a subject in a sentence.

=> I will go hunting
Hunting is a gerund that serves as a direct Object

=> My mom's hobby is singing
Singing serves as a gerund to complement the subject "my mom's hobby".

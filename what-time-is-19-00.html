19. QUIZ B INGRISWHAT TIME IS IT?- ( 10:00 ) - ( 03:08 )- ( 02:39)- ( 05 : 30)Jangan ngasal...​

WHAT TIME IS IT?

(Pukul Berapakah itu?)

10:0003:0802:3905:30

TipsMenit 1-29 gunakan (menit + past + jam berada)Menit 31-59 gunakan (sisa menit + to + jam menuju)Menit ke 15 gunakan (a quarter past + jam berada)Menit ke 45 gunakan (a quarter to + jam menuju)Menit ke 30 gunakan (half past + jam berada)Jika tidak ada menit, gunakan (jam berada + o'clock)Jawaban

10:00

⤷ It's ten o'clock (pukul 10 tepat)

Tidak ada menit ➔ (jam berada + o'clock)Jam berada = 10 (ten)Jawaban = Ten o'clock

03:08

⤷ It's eight past three (pukul 3 lewat 8)

Menit 1-29 ➔ (menit + past + jam berada)Menit = 8 (eight)Jam berada = 3 (three)Jawaban = Eight past three

02:39

⤷ It's twenty-one to three (pukul 3 kurang 21)

Menit 31-59 ➔ (sisa menit + to + jam menuju)Sisa menit = 60-39= 21 (twenty-one)Jam menuju = 3 (three)Jawaban = Twenty-one to three

05:30

⤷ It's half past five (pukul setengah 6)

Menit 30 ➔ (half past + jam berada)Jam berada = 5 (five)Jawaban = Half past five

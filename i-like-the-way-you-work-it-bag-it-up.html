dlm penyebutan adjective order, ada urutannya

yaitu:

O S A Sh C O M P eN

apa itu OSAShCOMP?

O: opinion. ex, great, good, heavy, etc.

S: Size. ex, big, small, etc.

A: Age. ex, old, young, new, etc

Sh: Shape. ex, square, round, etc.

C: colour. ex. red, blue, etc

O: origin. ex. javanese, australian, etc.

M: material. ex, rubber, plastic, etc

P: purpose. ex, for studying, for riding

eN: noun/kata benda

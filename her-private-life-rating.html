13. permutasi dari -private - recruiter Note : sama siapa sajakah yang memiliki rank private recruiter?


____________________

Rumus:

Permutasi (jika ada unsur ganda) => P = N!÷K!

Permutasi (jika tanpa unsur ganda) => P = N!

Keterangan:

N = Jumlah Unsur

K = Unsur Ganda

P = Permutasi

___________________

PENYELESAIAN:

Diketahui:

PrivateN = 7

Ditanyakan:

P = ...?

Jawab:

P = N!

P = 7!

P = 7×6×5×4×3×2×1

P = 5.040

•••

Diketahui:

RecruiterN = 9K = 3 (R), 2 (E)

Ditanyakan:

P = ...?

Jawab:

P = N!/K!

P = 9!/3!×2!

P = 9×8×7×6×5×4×3×2×1/3×2×1×2×1

P = 362.880/12

P = 30.240

KESIMPULAN:Private = 5.040 susunanRecruiter = 30.240 susunan

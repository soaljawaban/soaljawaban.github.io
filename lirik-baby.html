13. Is the baby drinking a milk now? a. Yes, the baby does b. Yes, the baby do c. Yes, the baby did d. Yes, the baby is


d. Yes, the baby is

Penjelasan:

(?) To be + S + V-ing

Responses

☑ Yes, + S + to be[x] No, + S + to be + not

Contoh:

Are they eating bread now?

☑ Yes, they are[x] No, they are not

Is the baby drinking a milk now?

☑ Yes, the baby is[x] No, the baby is notD. Yes, the baby is

Karena memakai present continous tense yang strukturnya subject + to be + verb-ing
